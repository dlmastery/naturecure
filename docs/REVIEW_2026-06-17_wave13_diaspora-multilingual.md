### 3.3 Counterfeit-supplement-verification (six-step framework)

**WHO Member State Mechanism on Substandard and Falsified (SF) Medical Products** + **VigiBase** + **EU Falsified Medicines Directive 2011/62/EU + 2D-Datamatrix** + **AYUSH GMP + IPC** + **NMPA** + **TGA SUSMP** = multi-jurisdiction authentication frame. WHO SF 2022 estimates ~10% of medicines in LMIC markets are substandard/falsified; for grey-market diaspora-sourced supplements (especially traditional-medicine formulas without batch-level chain-of-custody) prevalence is higher. Documented cases: heavy-metal contamination in non-GMP Ayurvedic bhasma (Saper 2008 + 2024 JAMA series — methodological provenance only, US-academic-lineage exception applies); steroid-adulteration in TCM kidney-tonics (NMPA 2018 + 2022 enforcement); undeclared sildenafil/tadalafil in male-vitality herbals (TGA 2019-2024); undeclared sibutramine in weight-loss "naturals" (EU RAPEX 2020-2024).
