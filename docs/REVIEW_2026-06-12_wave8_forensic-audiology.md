## 1. AUDIOGRAM-FRAUD + MALINGERING DETECTION — THE DOSSIER'S SELF-ADMINISTERED SCORING IS MEDICO-LEGALLY DEFENCELESS

### 1.1 What the dossier currently ships

§11.2 names the Tinnitus Functional Index (TFI, Meikle 2012 — derived in a US veteran cohort, 13-point MCID), Tinnitus Handicap Inventory (THI, Newman 1996), 0-10 daily loudness/annoyance slider, PSQI, ISI, GAD-7, PHQ-9, consumer audiogram (Mimi / SonicCloud / EarTrumpet), pitch/loudness match (TinnitusPlay), 7-day home BP average. §11.3 names sixteen DTC comorbidity labs. The framing throughout is **strictly self-administered**, with the (wave-3-corrected) caveat that smartphone audiograms miss mid-frequency notch loss 30-50% of the time and cannot distinguish conductive from sensorineural — but the corrective is "get a clinical audiogram + tympanometry + bone-conduction + acoustic reflex thresholds before purchasing aids," framed as a hearing-aid-purchase gate, not as a medico-legal evidence-grade requirement.

### 1.2 Why the self-administered framing is a medico-legal vulnerability

In any compensation-claim or PI litigation, the claimant's tinnitus severity is a contested fact determining award magnitude. TFI / THI / 0-10 slider are **single-source self-report with no malingering-detection layer**. Compensation litigation has, for ~60 years, developed a parallel **objective-audiology ladder** because subjective tinnitus + hearing-loss reports are known to be malingered or amplified. The load-bearing court-accepted markers:

- **Stenger test (BSA recommended procedure 2016).** Two-tone cross-check for unilateral / asymmetric claims; if claimant denies hearing in affected ear but Stenger reverses lateralisation, claim is invalidated. Used by NHS medico-legal audiology + Australian WorkCover panels.
- **Doerfler-Stewart procedure.** Pure-tone-in-noise threshold-shift cross-check exposing inconsistent voluntary thresholds; routine in UK MoD Defence Audiology medico-legal disputes.
- **DPOAE (Distortion Product Otoacoustic Emissions).** Objective measure of cochlear outer-hair-cell function; DPOAE present in a frequency band where claimant reports profound loss is **organic-inconsistent** — court-accepted across all major jurisdictions.
- **ABR wave-I/V latencies + ASSR.** Objective electrophysiology of cochlear nerve + brainstem; ABR thresholds within 10-15 dB of behavioural = organic-consistent; 30+ dB better than behavioural = non-organic / functional / malingered. Court-accepted UK + EU + AU + IN since the 1990s.
- **Cortical N1-P2 evoked thresholds.** Modern objective cortical-level hearing thresholds in adults; used in UK NHS + Indian AIIMS forensic-audiology when ABR is ambiguous.
- **Lombard sign + acoustic-reflex consistency.** Lower-level cross-checks for grossly inconsistent presentations.

**None is named in the dossier.** §11.2 presents the TFI as primary, surrounded by self-administered overlays. Appropriate for patient-tracking; **medico-legally inadequate** for any user who will later document severity in a compensation context — and the dossier signals nothing about banking objective-audiology evidence in parallel.

### 1.3 The asymmetry in real claims

In UK IIDB (DWP / Social Security Contributions and Benefits Act 1992 + IIAC reports), a tinnitus + NIHL claim runs Adjudicator → Medical Board → First-tier Tribunal → Upper Tribunal. At each step the claimant's audiogram + tinnitus severity is tested against: **pre-employment audiogram baseline**; **audiometric configuration consistency** (NIHL = 3-6 kHz notch; presbyacusis = high-frequency slope; acoustic trauma = sudden onset; vestibular schwannoma + Ménière + autoimmune + ototoxic carry distinct configurations); **symmetry vs asymmetry**; **speech-discrimination consistency** (speech scores out of proportion to pure-tone thresholds = functional / central contributor); **tinnitus pitch-match + loudness-match consistency over visits**. A claimant arriving at a Medical Board with only NicheCore-grade self-administered TFI + a Mimi smartphone audiogram is **structurally disadvantaged** vs one with banked NHS-clinic-grade tympanometry + bone-conduction + DPOAE + ABR + speech-in-noise. The dossier currently signals nothing about the second pathway.

### 1.4 Specific edits

- **Edit 1.1.** Add **§11.2.5 "Objective audiology — the medico-legal evidence layer."** ~350 words: §11.2 self-administered scoring is for patient-tracking, inadequate for compensation / PI / MVA / industrial-deafness; objective ladder named — DPOAE, ABR + ASSR, cortical N1-P2, Stenger, Doerfler-Stewart, Lombard, acoustic-reflex consistency, speech-in-noise + speech-discrimination consistency, tympanometry + bone-conduction; pre-employment baseline preservation; honest framing that all are clinic-grade (NHS UK, EU public, Hearing Australia + WorkCover panels AU, Command Hospital / AIIMS / KIMS IN — service-pathway citation per wave-7) — home OTC stack does not substitute.
- **Edit 1.2.** Add **§11.3 row** "Pre-employment + pre-injury baseline audiogram preservation." Anyone with noise-exposure occupation (military, construction, manufacturing, mining, agriculture, music, emergency services), MVA head/neck injury, or sudden acute-noise-injury banks clinical-grade audiogram + DPOAE + tympanometry within 4-12 weeks as medico-legal baseline. Precaution + evidence-preservation, not protocol-prerequisite.
- **Edit 1.3.** Update **§11.5 life-stage gate** with row: *"Active or anticipated tinnitus compensation claim (UK IIDB / EU workmen's-comp / AU WorkCover / IN ECA 2009 / MVA-tinnitus / industrial-deafness PI) — supplement self-tracking with clinical-grade objective audiology banked as medico-legal evidence. See §11.2.5."*

### 1.5 Verdict on Dimension 1

**Major revision.** The dossier's self-administered scoring framing is right for patient-education but **medico-legally vulnerable** for the meaningful sub-population of tinnitus users who are or will be claimants. ## 2. COMPENSATION FRAMEWORK — UK IIDB + EU NATIONAL + AUSTRALIAN WORKCOVER + INDIAN ECA + MOTOR VEHICLES ACT — INVISIBLE IN THE DOSSIER

### 2.1 The dossier has no compensation-framework signal

Searching the dossier for "compensation", "IIDB", "industrial injuries", "workmen's compensation", "WorkCover", "personal injury", "tariff", "ECA 2009", "tribunal", "claim", "settlement" returns **near-zero hits**. The wave-7 review surfaced the veteran-system pathway (UK MoD AFCS + US VA + AU DVA + IN AFMS + IL IDF) and proposed a §10.7.6 veteran-system addition. That fix is necessary but **insufficient** — it covers the service-connected sub-cohort. The **civilian industrial-deafness + civilian PI + civilian MVA-tinnitus sub-cohort** is materially larger across the OECD and is invisible.

### 2.2 The five major civilian compensation frameworks

- **UK IIDB + IIAC schedule.** Tinnitus + NIHL recognised under IIAC Prescribed Disease A10 (Occupational Deafness) since 1975 (refined IIAC reports 1990, 2002, 2018). Minimum 20% disability for award; ~£40-£200/week as of 2026. Pathway: DWP Form BI 100A → Adjudicator → Medical Board → First-tier Tribunal (Social Entitlement Chamber) → Upper Tribunal. Audiometric pattern: bilateral SNHL with 4 kHz notch.
- **UK civil PI under CPR 1998 Parts 35 + 16 + Pre-Action Protocols.** Industrial-deafness lineage: Thompson v Smiths Shiprepairers [1984] AC 405; Berry v Stone Manganese Marine 1972; Brookes v South Yorkshire PTE 2005; Baker v Quantum Clothing Group [2011] UKSC 17. Damages: £8k-£40k moderate; £40k-£250k+ severe with loss-of-amenity + future-care.
- **EU national workmen's-comp.** Germany BK 2301 (Lärmschwerhörigkeit) + BK 2102 (mechanical-trauma); claim via Berufsgenossenschaft + HNO-Gutachten. France Tableau 42 + 98; CPAM pathway. Netherlands WAO/WIA. Spain INSS. Italy INAIL.
- **Australian state WorkCover + WorkSafe.** SafeWork NSW, WorkCover QLD, WorkSafe Vic, ReturnToWorkSA, WorkCover WA + Tas + ACT + NT. Tariffs via AMA Guides 6th edition state-adapted. Comcare for federal workers.
- **Indian ECA 2009 + Motor Vehicles Act 1988 § 166 + ESIC + CPA 2019.** ECA covers employer-attributable NIHL + traumatic tinnitus via Workmen's Compensation Commissioner + Schedule I tariff. MVA whiplash-tinnitus via Motor Accident Claims Tribunal (MACT) covering pain-and-suffering + medical + loss-of-earnings. ESIC for ESI employees. CPA 2019 for product-liability cross-claims.

### 2.3 The claimant-user-base intersection

Wave-7 estimated 5-7M veterans on tinnitus compensation rolls across the OECD. **The civilian claimant base is materially larger:** UK ~14M occupationally noise-exposed (HSE 2023), ~17k new NIHL claims/year (DWP 2024); EU ~40-50M noise-exposed (EU-OSHA 2021); AU ~1.5M (Safe Work Australia 2024); IN ~80-100M (ICMR 2023, factoring informal sector). A non-trivial fraction of any tinnitus consumer-platform's user base is in active claim, between claims, or pre-claim — the dossier's silence is a structural gap.

### 2.4 Why this matters even in a strict OTC + home + sovereignty doctrine

The doctrine excludes recommending prescription drugs, clinic-based treatments, or US-regulator authority citations. **It does not exclude surfacing the compensation-system pathways a user is entitled to.** The omission of these pathways is functionally a sovereignty-doctrine violation in the opposite direction — the dossier presents the user as fully financially-capable (£390-£680/mo full protocol), when in reality a significant fraction of users are either entitled to compensation that the dossier could surface or are in active litigation where the dossier's framing affects their case. **This is the same logic the wave-7 review used for veteran-system pathway**, extended to the civilian claimant cohort.

### 2.5 Specific edits

- **Edit 2.1.** Add **§10.7.7 "Civilian compensation pathway."** ~500 words: UK IIDB IIAC PD A10 + civil PI under CPR Part 35; EU national workmen's-comp; Australian state WorkCover + WorkSafe + AMA Guides 6th edition; Indian ECA 2009 + Motor Vehicles Act § 166 MACT + ESIC + CPA 2019. Cover compensation offered, application pathway, realistic timelines (6-36 months UK IIDB; 12-60 months UK civil PI; 6-24 months IN MACT), audiometric evidence required (cross-reference Dimension 1 clinical-vs-self asymmetry), specialist medico-legal audiology referral (NHS UK; HNO-Gutachten DE; AU panels; AIIMS/KEM IN). Frame as pathway disclosure, doctrine pattern matching AYUSH / AIIMS / EMA / wave-7 veteran-system.

See §10.7.7. Bank clinical-grade audiology in parallel (§11.2.5)."*

## 3. EXPERT-WITNESS REPORT STANDARDS + DOSSIER'S "HONEST PLURALIST EVIDENCE" FRAMING — EXTRACTABLE IN CROSS-EXAMINATION

### 3.1 The dossier's pluralist-evidence framing as a double-edged sword

The dossier's signature achievement (post-wave-2 biostatistics + wave-5 fact-checker + wave-6 psychometrician corrections) is **graded honest evidence** — Ginkgo demoted to C with Cochrane null surfaced honestly; Mg restated as cofactor-floor not chronic-tinnitus treatment with Cederroth 2011 null; Lenire demoted to D with no-sham-comparator surfaced; Bhramari demoted to H with classical-lineage but only pilot-tinnitus evidence; CBT-T / MBCT-T demoted from A to B per honest GRADE reading; six traditions (Ayurveda, TCM, Unani, Siddha, Tibetan, Homeopathy) graded T with classical-lineage acknowledged + modern-trial absence acknowledged; Lipoflavonoid not endorsed; homeopathy explicitly NOT recommended for active use per Simpson 1998 RCT. **This is exceptional patient-education content.** It is **also a cross-examination weapon** in adversarial PI litigation.

### 3.2 How a defendant insurer's barrister will use this

In UK CPR Part 35 PI litigation, a defendant's barrister cross-examining the claimant's medical-expert witness can quote from the dossier:

- **Cochrane found Ginkgo null on primary tinnitus** — to undermine "treatment-resistant" claims.
- **~50-80% of acute tinnitus resolves spontaneously at 6 months** (Davis & Refaie 2000; Yang 2017) — to argue the claimant's tinnitus would have resolved regardless and the defendant's exposure is not proximate cause.
- **Placebo-response decomposition (15-30% double-blind sham; 35-50% open-label; honest baseline ~40-50% placebo-style responder)** — to argue reported improvement is placebo, not protocol-attributable.
- **§13.7 grade D/T/H ratings** — to argue the claimant's OTC stack expenditure is not "reasonable medical treatment cost" recoverable under CPR PI Pre-Action Protocol.
- **§11.7 "most of what you measure as improvement in months 1-3 is expectancy + ritual + natural-history"** — to argue self-reported improvement is not protocol-attributable.

**Every quotation is from a public document the claimant is plausibly reading.** The dossier becomes admissible-grade evidence under CPR Part 35; Indian CPC § 45; Australian Evidence Act 1995 § 79.

### 3.3 The symmetric framing

The same honest evidence framing also **discharges the defendant's duty-of-care** under Hedley Byrne v Heller [1964] AC 465 + Caparo Industries v Dickman [1990] 2 AC 605. If the defendant can show a reasonable claimant + expert would have read the dossier and adjusted expectations, the defendant's argument that continued out-of-pocket treatment is unreasonable is strengthened. **NicheCore as publisher cannot be the claimant's advocate — but the dossier's framing should not actively work against the claimant either.**

### 3.4 What expert-witness reports actually require

CPR Part 35 + Practice Direction 35 require: expert qualifications; substance of instructions; literature + methodology; range of opinion; conclusions; statement of compliance + truth. The dossier's structure — graded evidence ladder + §13.7 comparison + multi-source citation — is **substantively close** to a properly-instructed audiology expert-witness report. **What is missing is the explicit forensic-audiology framing letting a claimant + claimant's expert use it positively.**

## 4. §13.7 EVIDENCE-TIER COMPARISON + PERSONAL-INJURY CLAIM WORKFLOW DEFENSIBILITY

### 4.1 The §13.7 table is admissible-grade evidence — but structurally biased

§13.7 lists ~25 approaches with grade + plain-words verdict + where-it-fits column. The table is **substantively close to CPR Part 35 Practice Direction 35.10 + Daubert v Merrell Dow 1993 (US Federal — excluded from doctrine but the methodology is universal) + Frye v United States 1923 (general-acceptance test, EU + AU + IN parallel via expert-opinion admissibility rules) standards** for what an expert-witness report should look like. **However**, every column in §13.7 is **subjective-outcome-anchored**: distress, loudness, "real relief," responder-rate. **None** of the §13.7 entries surface the **objective-audiology-evidence ladder** (DPOAE / ABR / tympanometry / speech-in-noise / pre-injury baseline / audiometric configuration consistency) that compensation + PI workflows require as the load-bearing evidence layer.

This means: a claimant's expert witness who tries to use §13.7 as their evidence base in a PI report **will be cross-examined to "where is the objective-audiology evidence backing the claimant's subjective tinnitus severity?"** — and §13.7 provides no answer. The expert must reach outside the dossier to clinical-audiology textbooks (Katz's Handbook of Clinical Audiology; Stach's Clinical Audiology) + medico-legal-audiology literature (BSA recommended procedures; Australasian Audiological Society medico-legal guidelines; Indian Society of Audiology forensic-audiology) for the objective-evidence layer.

The fix is a **parallel medico-legal evidence-tier table** placed alongside §13.7 — same grading discipline indexed to the objective-audiology evidence ladder rather than subjective-outcome. Not a retraction of §13.7; the **complementary layer** the compensation/PI user needs alongside patient-treatment evidence.

### 4.3 What the complementary table covers

## 5. WRONGFUL-PRESCRIPTION / PUBLISHER-LIABILITY / NEGLIGENT-MISSTATEMENT EXPOSURE — THE DOSSIER NEEDS A §16.5 LIABILITY-MITIGATION SURFACE

### 5.1 The liability-exposure question

If a NicheCore reader follows the protocol — full §8.1-§8.5 stack + §5 devices + §6 dietary framework + §7 lifestyle anchors — and suffers a foreseeable harm (e.g., warfarin user follows Combo 1 with Vit K2 MK-7 despite the warfarin-flag and has a stroke; Hashimoto's user follows §8.4 Ashwagandha + levothyroxine without TSH monitoring and develops thyrotoxic crisis; CKD-5 user follows §3.3 magnesium without eGFR-gating and develops hypermagnesaemic cardiac arrest; pulsatile-tinnitus user follows the home stack instead of vascular imaging and a missed dural fistula bleeds), **what is NicheCore's legal exposure as the publisher of the protocol**?

The dossier currently has extensive safety scaffolding — the top-of-page red-flag block covers CI / pulsatile / vestibular-schwannoma / SSHL / warfarin / CKD; §10.6 strictly-avoid list; §11.5 life-stage gate; §11.6 who-this-protocol-is-not-for; the §10.1-§10.6 interaction matrix. **This is substantively excellent safety architecture**. It does not, however, **explicitly discharge the publisher's duty-of-care obligation** under the applicable legal frameworks. The fix is a §16.5 liability-mitigation surface that explicitly names the doctrine + the audit trail.

### 5.2 The applicable legal frameworks

- **UK common-law tort.** Donoghue v Stevenson [1932] AC 562 — neighbour-principle duty to consumers of published products. Hedley Byrne v Heller [1964] AC 465 — negligent-misstatement duty for published advice. Caparo Industries v Dickman [1990] 2 AC 605 — three-stage test (foreseeability + proximity + fair-just-reasonable). Bolam v Friern HMC [1957] 1 WLR 582 + Bolitho v City and Hackney HA [1997] 3 WLR 1151 — standard-of-care + logical-defensibility. Consumer Rights Act 2015 — storefront product-liability.
- **EU.** Council Directive 85/374/EEC product-liability (national transposition); Directive 2001/95/EC general product safety; GDPR 2016/679 — health-data.
- **India.** Consumer Protection Act 2019 — product-liability + service-deficiency via District/State/National Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission (NCDRC). IPC § 304-A (negligence death). Contract Act 1872 + Sale of Goods Act 1930 for storefront. IT Act 2000 + Reasonable Security Practices Rules 2011 for digital data.
- **Australia.** Australian Consumer Law (Schedule 2, Competition and Consumer Act 2010) — product safety + s 18 misleading-conduct + Part 3-5 product-liability. Rogers v Whitaker [1992] HCA 58 — duty to warn of material risks. Therapeutic Goods Act 1989 cross-over.
- **US (excluded from doctrine but accessible via US IPs):** First Amendment generally shields health-information publishers for non-specific information; Restatement (Third) Torts § 5 product-liability + § 4 manufacturing-defect. Liability-mitigation context only, not authority anchor.

### 5.3 The doctrine that discharges duty-of-care

A publisher discharges duty-of-care when it: (a) **identifies foreseeable risks explicitly** — §11.5 life-stage gate + §11.6 not-for-list + top-of-page red-flag block already do this; (b) **distinguishes general information from individualised advice** — needs explicit surface; (c) **provides material-risk warnings proportionate to risk** — §10.6 + §10.5.5 + warfarin/CKD/CI/pulsatile/SSHL red flags already do this; (d) **maintains audit trail of evidence-grading + revision history** — schemaVersion + factualErrorsFixedThisRevision + wave-2/3/4/5/6/7 audit-trail do this; (e) **cites authoritative sources with currency** — EMA + AYUSH + WHO + Cochrane + RCT discipline does this; (f) **disclaims professional medical relationship + recommends professional consultation** — partial; needs explicit surface.

### 5.4 What's missing

The architecture is 80% there. Missing is the **explicit consolidated liability-mitigation surface** an MDU / MPS / NCDRC / EU adjudicator would look for. The §16.5 fix consolidates: (a) publisher framing — NicheCore is evidence-graded patient-education + research-aggregation, not a medical practice; dossier is general information, not individualised advice; (b) sovereignty + user responsibility; (c) clinician-consultation recommendation for users on prescriptions + chronic conditions + §11.5 rows (sovereignty stands; this is duty-discharge, not doctrine retreat); (d) evidence-grading + audit-trail (schemaVersion + wave-1 through wave-8 + structured-citation roadmap); (e) storefront product-liability on parallel rails per UK CRA 2015 + EU 85/374/EEC + IN CPA 2019 + AU ACL (wave-3 supplement-QC discipline); (f) jurisdiction + governing-law + dispute-resolution surface (NCDRC IN; County Court / Trading Standards UK; EU national consumer-ombudsman; AU state Consumer Affairs).
