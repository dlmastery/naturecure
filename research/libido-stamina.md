## 1. Executive Summary

Sexual desire, arousal, performance and stamina are conventionally siloed by gender and treated with isolated drugs — PDE5-inhibitors for male ED, flibanserin/bremelanotide for female HSDD, testosterone for low-T-related loss, SSRIs for premature ejaculation, oestradiol cream for GSM-related dyspareunia. The integrative reframing is different: libido and stamina are the **whole-body signature of a four-node network shared by both sexes** — (i) **endothelial-vascular nitric oxide signalling** (the same NO pathway underlying erection in men and clitoral engorgement / vaginal lubrication in women, plus all-system cardiovascular stamina); (ii) **sex-hormone milieu** (testosterone in both sexes for desire, oestrogen for arousal + tissue function, progesterone balance, DHEA, prolactin, oxytocin); (iii) **HPA-axis + cortisol-DHEA balance** (chronic stress is the dominant modern libido suppressant in both sexes); and (iv) **mitochondrial-cardio-metabolic stamina** (VO₂max, capillary density, glycogen + fat-oxidation flexibility, cardio-vascular endothelial function). **Synthesis:** A natural, OTC, home-based protocol that simultaneously restores NO signalling (L-citrulline + L-arginine, beetroot, pomegranate, Pycnogenol, dark chocolate flavanols), rebuilds sex-hormone milieu (Ashwagandha, Maca, Tongkat ali male / Shatavari female, Tribulus cautious, Kapikacchu, Shilajit), corrects HPA-DHEA (Ashwagandha, Holy Basil, Yoga Nidra), and reinforces cardio-mitochondrial stamina (Zone 2 + HIIT + strength training, CoQ10, omega-3, creatine, beetroot) — without exogenous T, PDE5-inhibitor or flibanserin.

## 2. Mechanistic Network (4-node, both sexes)

**2.1 Endothelial-vascular NO signalling.** Endothelial nitric oxide synthase (eNOS) converts L-arginine → L-citrulline + NO. NO activates soluble guanylate cyclase → cGMP → smooth muscle relaxation → **erection (male), clitoral engorgement + vaginal lubrication (female), coronary + skeletal-muscle vasodilation (both)**. PDE5 degrades cGMP — sildenafil and tadalafil are PDE5 inhibitors. Endothelial dysfunction (oxidative stress, insulin resistance, smoking, sedentary lifestyle, age) crushes NO signalling. Targeted by **L-citrulline 3–6 g (preferable to L-arginine for sustained NO — citrulline bypasses gut arginase), beetroot juice (250 ml = 5 mmol nitrate), pomegranate juice/extract, Pycnogenol (pine bark) 200 mg, dark chocolate flavanols (85%+), Maca, Korean ginseng, omega-3 EPA+DHA, exercise (especially Zone 2 + HIIT)**. **Synthesis:** NO restoration is the foundational lever shared by both sexes.

**2.2 Sex-hormone milieu — testosterone, oestrogen, progesterone, DHEA, prolactin, oxytocin.** Testosterone drives libido in **both sexes** (women have testosterone too — total T 25–80 ng/dL is normal range; deficiency causes hypoactive sexual desire disorder, HSDD). Oestrogen maintains female arousal-related tissue function (vaginal lubrication, clitoral sensitivity, vaginal epithelium). DHEA-S is a precursor pool that declines with age in both sexes. High prolactin (stress, dopamine antagonists, certain SSRIs) suppresses libido. Oxytocin amplifies bonding, arousal, orgasm — released by touch, sex, breastfeeding, gazing. Targeted by **male: Ashwagandha + Tongkat ali + Shilajit (see Men's Testosterone dossier); female: Ashwagandha + Shatavari + Maca + DHEA where OTC and measured; both: Vitex (female-cautious), Kapikacchu (dopamine → prolactin↓), Boron (free T), Zinc, Vitamin D**. **Synthesis:** Hormonal floor restoration unlocks desire.

**2.3 HPA-axis + cortisol-DHEA balance — the dominant modern libido suppressant.** Chronic stress is responsible for more libido and stamina loss in modern populations than any other factor. Sustained cortisol suppresses GnRH → LH/FSH → testes/ovaries. Pregnenolone is shunted from sex steroids to cortisol ("pregnenolone steal"). Sleep deprivation amplifies cortisol and crushes T. DHEA-S falls with chronic stress. Sympathetic dominance suppresses parasympathetic-mediated arousal physiology. Targeted by **Ashwagandha KSM-66, Holy Basil, Rhodiola, Bacopa, Yoga Nidra, MBSR, pranayama, sleep work, partnered touch, vagal practices**. **Synthesis:** Without HPA correction, every other lever regresses.

**2.4 Mitochondrial-cardio-metabolic stamina.** VO₂max is one of the strongest predictors of all-cause mortality and a direct proxy for sexual stamina, recovery and overall vitality. Mitochondrial biogenesis (PGC-1α), capillary density, glycogen + fat-oxidation flexibility, insulin sensitivity, and endothelial function all govern how much "tank" a body has. Targeted by **Zone 2 cardio (3–4×/week, 45 min, 60–70% HRmax — capillary + mitochondrial), heavy strength training (3×/week — anabolic + mitochondrial + endothelial), HIIT (1–2×/week — VO₂max), CoQ10 ubiquinol, creatine 5 g, beetroot nitrate, omega-3, Vitamin D + K2, magnesium, B-complex, methylfolate, methyl-B12**. **Synthesis:** Without cardio-mitochondrial substrate, libido has no stage to perform on.
