## 1. Executive Summary

Cravings and emotional eating are conventionally siloed as "willpower failure" or treated with prescription combination drugs (naltrexone-bupropion, topiramate-phentermine) or therapy alone. The integrative reframing is different: cravings are the **brain-gut-glycaemic-microbiome-circadian network signal** that something upstream is misaligned, driven by four convergent root mechanisms — (i) HPA-cortisol dysregulation that wires reward + emotion toward calorie-dense palatable foods (sugar / fat / salt), (ii) dopamine-reward sensitisation to hyperpalatable industrial foods (sugar–fat–salt triumvirate) that hijack mesolimbic D1/D2 signalling, (iii) blood-sugar volatility + reactive hypoglycaemia that triggers acute carb cravings, and (iv) gut microbiome dysbiosis with low SCFA producers + *Candida*/yeast overgrowth + serotonin precursor depletion driving cravings via vagal-gut-brain signalling. **Synthesis:** A natural, OTC, home-based protocol that simultaneously dampens HPA, re-sensitises dopamine reward, stabilises glycaemia, rebuilds the microbiome, and addresses the underlying emotional/sleep substrate yields durable craving reduction — making cravings a signal you respond to rather than a force you resist.

## 2. Mechanistic Network (4-node)

**2.1 HPA-cortisol-emotional eating axis.** Chronic stress → elevated cortisol → ↑ NPY/AgRP (hunger orexigenic peptides) → preference for energy-dense palatable food + visceral fat storage. Acute stress activates amygdala → reward-seeking eating. The "comfort-food" loop is real and biochemical. Targeted by Ashwagandha KSM-66, Rhodiola, *Bacopa*, holy basil/Tulsi, L-theanine, magnesium glycinate, pranayama, Yoga Nidra, vagal practices, 4-7-8 breath, cold immersion, sufficient sleep. **Synthesis:** Without HPA modulation, no cognitive strategy holds against the biochemical pull.

**2.2 Dopamine-reward hyperpalatability sensitisation.** Industrial sugar-fat-salt food activates mesolimbic dopamine (D1/D2) more powerfully than whole foods. Repeated exposure reduces D2 receptor density (similar to substance addiction signatures on PET imaging). Cravings become incentive-salience driven. Targeted by elimination of industrial hyperpalatable foods (re-sensitisation in 30–60 days), Mucuna pruriens (natural L-DOPA), Rhodiola, *Gymnema sylvestre* (suppresses sweet taste — paradoxically retrains sweetness perception), Berberine, tyrosine, B6 + P-5-P, dopaminergic exercise (HIIT, cold immersion). **Synthesis:** Removing the stimulus + restoring dopamine substrate normalises the reward response.

**2.3 Blood-sugar volatility + reactive hypoglycaemia.** Refined carb spike → insulin overshoot → 2–4 h post-meal nadir → adrenergic + carb-craving response. CGM data (Stelo, Lingo, Levels, Nutrisense) consistently reveal personal trigger foods. Targeted by Berberine, Cinnamon, Gymnema, Fenugreek, Chromium, Inositol, ACV pre-meal, protein-first eating, fibre-first eating, sprouted Siri Dhanyalu millets, eliminating refined carbs / HFCS / industrial sweeteners. **Synthesis:** Stable glucose curve = quiet cravings.

**2.4 Gut microbiome / *Candida* / serotonin axis.** Dysbiotic gut with low SCFA producers + *Candida albicans* overgrowth signals sugar cravings via vagal afferents and via *Candida* biochemistry (the yeast literally "demands" sugar to feed). Low gut serotonin precursor (5-HTP from tryptophan) reduces satiety + mood. Targeted by sprouted millets + bitter greens + fermented foods + multi-strain probiotic + *Saccharomyces boulardii* (anti-*Candida*) + Berberine (anti-*Candida*) + Pau d'Arco + caprylic acid + tryptophan-rich foods (eggs, pumpkin seed, turkey, paneer) + 5-HTP if appropriate. **Synthesis:** Microbiome equilibrium is the foundation that lets HPA, dopamine and glucose stabilise.
