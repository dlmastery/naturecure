## 1. Executive Summary

Blood sugar dysregulation — from pre-diabetes through early type-2 diabetes to multi-medicated T2D — is conventionally siloed as a pharmaceutical disease treated by an escalating ladder (metformin → sulphonylurea / DPP-4 → SGLT-2 → GLP-1 → insulin) with lifestyle as a footnote. The integrative reframing is fundamentally different: dysglycaemia is the **whole-body metabolic-immune-microbiome network failure with iron-dependent β-cell ferroptosis on a permeable gut barrier**, driven by four convergent root mechanisms — (i) **iron-dependent lipid peroxidation (ferroptosis) of pancreatic β-cells** through Nrf2 / GPX4 / System Xc⁻ collapse under hyperglycaemic + lipotoxic load, (ii) **gliadin-zonulin gut permeability** and **LPS endotoxemia** chronically activating hepatic TLR4 / NF-κB and undermining insulin signalling at hepatocytes, adipocytes and skeletal muscle, (iii) **microbiome dysbiosis with depleted *Akkermansia muciniphila*, *Faecalibacterium prausnitzii*, *Blautia* and SCFA producers**, collapsing GPR41/43 Treg-mediated tolerance + butyrate-mediated AMPK signalling, and (iv) **circadian + HPA + autonomic dysregulation** with elevated cortisol, flattened cortisol curve, low HRV and sleep deprivation driving insulin resistance via SGK1, 11β-HSD1 and amplifying visceral storage. **Synthesis:** A natural, OTC, home-based protocol that simultaneously reinforces NRF2 / GPX4 to halt β-cell ferroptosis, seals the gut barrier, restores SCFA-producing flora, downregulates cortisol and rebuilds insulin sensitivity addresses upstream drivers — and yields HbA1c, FPG, HOMA-IR and ApoB drops that often allow medicated users to coordinate self-paced dose reductions with whoever prescribes their drug, and that allow pre-diabetic / hereditary-risk users to avoid the pharmaceutical ladder altogether.

## 2. Mechanistic Network (4-node)

**2.1 β-cell ferroptosis: Nrf2 / GPX4 / System Xc⁻ collapse.** **Ferroptosis** is an iron-dependent lipid-peroxidation cell death distinct from apoptosis or necrosis, characterised by the overwhelming accumulation of phospholipid hydroperoxides in cell membranes when **Glutathione Peroxidase 4 (GPX4)** is degraded. In T2D, chronic hyperglycaemia + lipotoxicity create an oxidative environment in the pancreas; iron deposition rises; β-cell glutathione reserves are exhausted; when GPX4 (a phospholipid hydroperoxidase that neutralises toxic lipid peroxides into harmless lipid alcohols) is downregulated, β-cells undergo ferroptotic death, leading to a permanent decline in insulin secretion. The same ferroptosis programme destroys melanocytes in vitiligo — explaining the documented epidemiological overlap of vitiligo and T2D. Targeted by NRF2 activators (Polydatin, Quercetin, Sulforaphane, *Polypodium leucotomos*, *Nigella sativa* thymoquinone) + glutathione cycle (NAC, ALA, selenium) + iron-burden reduction (donate blood if ferritin >100 ng/mL and male non-menstruating) + curcumin. **Synthesis:** Reinforcing GPX4 / NRF2 / System Xc⁻ is the upstream brake on β-cell loss — the same mechanism that protects melanocytes in vitiligo.

**2.2 Gut permeability: gliadin → zonulin → LPS endotoxemia → hepatic TLR4.** Gliadin binds CXCR3 on intestinal epithelium and triggers **zonulin** release; zonulin → MyD88 → EGFR / PAR2 → tight-junction disassembly. LPS from gram-negative gut bacteria translocates into portal circulation, activates hepatic TLR4 / NF-κB, induces hepatocyte insulin resistance (IRS-1 serine phosphorylation), drives hepatic de novo lipogenesis (SREBP-1c / ChREBP), and degrades skeletal-muscle and adipocyte insulin signalling. The result: insulin resistance + visceral adiposity + NAFLD/MASLD as a unified output of a leaky gut. Targeted by strict gluten elimination + zinc-carnosine + L-glutamine + DGL liquorice + slippery elm + marshmallow root + bone broth (glycine/collagen) + fermented foods + *Akkermansia*-supportive polyphenols (pomegranate, cranberry). **Synthesis:** No insulin-sensitising layer holds if gluten + seed-oil intake keeps the gate open.

**2.3 Microbiome dysbiosis + SCFA depletion + bile acid dysregulation.** Indian, Chinese, European and Australian metagenomic studies converge: T2D / pre-diabetic / hereditary-risk cohorts show depleted *Akkermansia muciniphila*, *Faecalibacterium prausnitzii*, *Blautia*, *Roseburia*, *Bifidobacterium* and expanded *Proteobacteria*, *Enterobacteriaceae* and methanogenic archaea. Loss of butyrate / propionate / acetate reduces GPR41/43 Treg signalling, blunts AMPK activation in skeletal muscle and impairs hepatic FXR / bile-acid axis. *Momordica charantia* complex polysaccharides cultivate beneficial bacteria, shifting metabolism markers ~11.95% toward insulin sensitivity. **Yu Quan Wan** (Jade Spring Pill, classical TCM with *Astragalus / Dioscorea*) and **Xiao Ke Fang** (Wasting-Thirst Formula, *Trichosanthes / Ophiopogon*) act on AMPK + β-cell protection respectively. Targeted by *Momordica charantia* polysaccharides + sprouted Siri Dhanyalu millets (foxtail / barnyard / kodo / little / browntop — Dr. Khadar Vali's revival) + fermented foods + multi-strain probiotic + Berberine (microbiome shift toward *Akkermansia*). **Synthesis:** SCFA recovery is the underlying immune-metabolic lever — the same lever that calms vitiligo-associated autoimmunity.

**2.4 HPA / circadian / autonomic dysregulation.** Chronic stress, shift work, late-night eating, blue-light exposure and poor sleep flatten cortisol, raise visceral fat via 11β-HSD1, lower HRV, blunt vagal tone and amplify insulin resistance via SGK1 / glucocorticoid signalling. Trauma + depression + anxiety bidirectionally feed dysglycaemia. Targeted by Ashwagandha KSM-66 (−23 to −28% cortisol across RCTs), Tulsi (*Ocimum sanctum*), *Bacopa*, *Rhodiola* (AM only), pranayama, Yoga Nidra, vagal practices, home cold immersion, sufficient sleep, AM sunlight, blue-light cut-off, 18 °C bedroom. **Synthesis:** Without HPA / circadian normalisation, the other three nodes regress.

### 6.3 Preparation methodology

**Sprouting protocol** (millets, mung, Bengal gram, fenugreek): rinse → soak filtered water 8–10 h → drain → cover muslin → 25–28 °C for 12–18 h until tail-sprouts emerge → rinse twice/day. Refrigerate ≤48 h. **Reduces phytic acid 30–50%, surges GABA, multiplies bioavailable B-vitamins, lowers glycaemic index 20–35%.**

**Kanji** (probiotic ferment): beetroot 200 g + carrot 100 g + mustard seed 5 g + black salt + 1 L filtered water → ferment 3–5 days at 20–25 °C.

**Dosa batter (gluten-free):** soaked Siri Dhanyalu (foxtail + little) 2 cups + urad dal ½ cup → wet-grind → ferment 12 h.

**Vijaysar wood tumbler** (classical Kerala *Madhumeha* tool): pour 200 ml water in a *Pterocarpus marsupium* wood tumbler at night → drink fasted in AM; wood polyphenols leach overnight; replace wood every 3 months.

**Ghee tempering** (anuvasana) of bitter greens with cumin and turmeric — enhances fat-soluble vitamin and curcumin absorption.

**ACV pre-meal** (1 tbsp in 200 ml water with straw, before main meal) — post-prandial glucose AUC −15 to −25% in cohort studies (Japanese + Swedish + Indian).

**Bone broth** (12 h slow simmer) — glycine, collagen, electrolytes, gut-lining support; 250 ml in lieu of mid-afternoon snack.
