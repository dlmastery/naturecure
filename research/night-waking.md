## 1. Executive Summary

Night-waking insomnia (sleep-maintenance insomnia, WASO > 30 min, sleep efficiency <85%) is conventionally siloed as a "sleep maintenance" problem treated with extended-release Z-drugs, doxepin and DORAs. The integrative reframing is different: middle-of-the-night awakenings are the **physiological signature of a metabolic-endocrine-thermoregulatory destabilisation that wins out over the maintenance machinery of sleep**, driven by four convergent root mechanisms — (i) nocturnal hypoglycaemia → reactive cortisol/glucagon/adrenaline surge at 02:00–04:00, (ii) excessive cortisol awakening response shifted earlier (CAR begins at 02:00–03:00 instead of 05:00–06:00), (iii) thermoregulatory dysfunction (hot flashes, warm bedroom, supine reflux, OSA-driven micro-arousals), and (iv) anxiety-rumination relapse at 03:00 with default-mode network re-ignition once light sleep arrives. **Synthesis:** An OTC, home-based protocol that stabilises overnight blood glucose, dampens the early-morning cortisol surge, restores thermal/respiratory comfort, and provides a 03:00 fallback re-entry path addresses the upstream drivers — not the sedation endpoint.

## 2. Mechanistic Network (4-node)

**2.1 Nocturnal hypoglycaemia / metabolic instability.** Late carb-light dinners, alcohol, fasting too aggressively, or insulin resistance cause liver glycogen to deplete around 02:00–04:00. Counter-regulatory cortisol + glucagon + adrenaline surge wakes the user (often with hunger or sweating). Key actors: hepatic glycogen reserves, glucagon-α-cell, adrenal cortisol, sympathetic adrenaline. Targeted by **balanced dinner with slow carbs + protein + fat**, optional **complex-carb HS snack** (banana + nut butter, 30 g sweet potato + 1 tsp ghee) for prone users, berberine and metabolic-stabilising supplements, CGM monitoring (Levels, Lingo, Stelo, Veri, NutriSense). **Synthesis:** Stable nocturnal glucose is non-negotiable for sleep maintenance.

**2.2 Cortisol awakening response (CAR) phase-shifted earlier.** Normally cortisol rises sharply 30 min before/after waking (05:00–06:00). HPA hyperactivity shifts this surge to 02:00–04:00, producing the classic "wake up wired at 3 AM" presentation. Drivers: chronic stress, late caffeine, alcohol withdrawal at 03:00, evening blue light, late HIIT. Targeted by Ashwagandha KSM-66, *Bacopa*, magnolia/honokiol, phosphatidylserine 100 mg pre-bed, evening Yoga Nidra, 4-7-8 breath at re-awakening, low-dose physiological melatonin 0.3 mg + extended-release option (Pure Encaps Melatonin SRT). **Synthesis:** Without HPA recalibration the 03:00 surge keeps firing.

**2.3 Thermoregulatory / respiratory disruption.** Core body temperature minimum normally falls to ~36 °C at 04:00; failure to drop (warm bedroom, dense bedding, peri-menopausal vasomotor symptoms, hot bath too close to bed, alcohol vasodilation) causes micro-arousals. OSA produces 5–30 wakings/hour via O₂ desaturation. GERD wakes supine sleepers within 60–180 min of dinner. RLS produces frequent micro-arousals with leg movement. Targeted by **bedroom 16–18 °C, mattress-cooling pad (ChiliPad, Eight Sleep Pod), breathable cotton/linen, wicking sleepwear, head-of-bed elevation 15° for GERD, Mandibular Advancement Device (MAD) consumer-OTC versions like SleepTight/SnoreRx for mild OSA + snoring, weight loss for moderate OSA, lateral-sleep training**. **Synthesis:** A warm body + warm room is the surest path to a 03:00 wake-up.

**2.4 Anxiety-rumination relapse at 03:00.** Once awake at 03:00, default-mode network re-ignites with planning + worry + replaying. Sleep onset for the second time is harder than the first because cortisol is now rising, melatonin is falling. Targeted by **stimulus control** (leave bed after 20 min, dim red light in another room, read paper book, return when sleepy), 4-7-8 breath, Bhramari, cognitive shuffle, Yoga Nidra audio at low volume, optional 100 mg L-theanine + 0.5 mg magnesium glycinate or 50 mg apigenin **PRN bedside re-dose**, journaling worry-parking-lot pre-bed. **Synthesis:** Behavioural and somatic anchors are the answer once awake — not staring at the ceiling.

## 5. Biophysical Interventions — home-administered only

**StressSleep / Brain-Mood domain — every device is consumer-purchasable and self-administrable. No clinic polysomnography requirement, no in-lab sleep study mandate.**
