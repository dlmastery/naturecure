## 1. Executive Summary

Tinnitus is conventionally siloed as either "nerve damage, nothing to do" or, in severe cases, as off-label gabapentin / tricyclic / benzodiazepine management — none of which target the upstream drivers, all of which carry meaningful side-effect and dependence liabilities. The integrative reframing: subjective tinnitus is the perceptual expression of **four convergent drivers** — (i) **cochlear oxidative + excitotoxic injury** (NMDA overactivation, glutathione depletion, hair-cell ferroptosis), (ii) **central gain amplification** in the dorsal cochlear nucleus + auditory cortex ("phantom-limb of the ear"), (iii) **cochlear + cerebrovascular insufficiency**, and (iv) **somatic / autonomic dysregulation** (cervical-TMJ, vagal hypotonia, sleep-stress loop). **Synthesis:** A natural, OTC, home-based protocol that protects hair cells, dampens central gain, restores cochlear perfusion and corrects somatic-autonomic drivers addresses the upstream causes — independent of whatever drug class the user may or may not be on.

## 2. Mechanistic Network (4-node)

**2.1 Cochlear oxidative + excitotoxic injury.** Noise, ototoxic drugs (loop diuretics, aminoglycosides, cisplatin, chronic high-dose NSAIDs/salicylates) and aging deplete cochlear glutathione, raise mitochondrial ROS in outer hair cells, and overactivate NMDA receptors on Type-I afferents — the synaptopathy model (Kujawa-Liberman; Manchester + Karolinska corroboration). Targeted by **NAC** (glutathione), **Mg glycinate/threonate** (NMDA — Israeli IDF Attias 1994 RCT in noise-induced tinnitus), **Zinc** (cochlear Zn²⁺ — Turkish Arda 2003 RCT in Zn-deficient subgroup), **CoQ10**, **R-ALA**, **methylcobalamin** (Israeli Shemesh 1993 — deficient subgroup), and ototoxic-exposure avoidance. **Synthesis:** Hair-cell preservation is the upstream brake.

**2.2 Central gain amplification + auditory cortex maladaptive plasticity.** When cochlear input drops, the dorsal cochlear nucleus and auditory cortex up-regulate gain to "find" the missing signal — and create a phantom one (Eggermont / Roberts model). Fusiform cells of the DCN integrate trigeminal (V) and C2-C3 dorsal-column input — the mechanism of somatic modulation. Targeted by **sound therapy** (broadband, notch-filtered — German Hannover Pantev/Okamoto + Tinnitracks; fractal — ReSound Relief, Oticon SoundSupport), **TRT — Jastreboff protocol** self-applied, **MBCT-Tinnitus** (UK Bristol Cima/McKenna RCTs), **Lion's Mane** (NGF/BDNF — mechanistic), and **Bhramari pranayama** (vibratory + vagal). **Synthesis:** Habituation, not pharmacology, is the durable perceptual lever.

**2.3 Cochlear + cerebrovascular insufficiency.** The cochlea is among the most metabolically demanding tissues per gram; the stria vascularis depends on a single end-arterial supply (labyrinthine artery from AICA) with no collaterals. Hypertension, dyslipidaemia, smoking and endothelial NO collapse drive vascular tinnitus. Targeted by **Ginkgo EGb 761 / Tebonin** (German RCTs Drewelow 2013, Procháska 2009; EMA HMPC monograph 2015), **Pycnogenol** (Italian Pescara Belcaro 2014/2017), **Vinpocetine** (Hungarian Cavinton data), **L-arginine + L-citrulline**, **dietary nitrate** (beetroot), **cocoa flavanols**, and Zone 2 cardio. **Synthesis:** Without cochlear perfusion, nothing else delivers.

**2.4 Somatic / autonomic dysregulation + cervical-TMJ inputs.** Up to ~40% of tinnitus is somatically modulable — pitch/loudness change with jaw clench, temple pressure, neck rotation, C1-C3 palpation. Vagal hypotonia (low HRV) and HPA dysregulation amplify perceived loudness; sleep fragmentation feeds the loop. Targeted by **cervical mobility + suboccipital release**, **TMJ release + bruxism night-guard**, **home cervical traction** (Saunders), **HRV biofeedback** (HeartMath, Sensate), **side-sleeping with unaffected ear up**, **Yoga Nidra**, **Bhramari + Nadi Shodhana**. **Synthesis:** The somatic-autonomic lever often delivers the largest TFI drops and is the lever most tinnitus sufferers have never been offered.

## 5. Biophysical Interventions — home-administered only

**Brain / Ear domain — every device is consumer-purchasable and self-administrable. No TMS, no clinic neuromodulation, no surgical decompression, no cochlear implant, no Botox-for-myoclonus, no intratympanic injection.**

**5.1 Sound therapy (PRIMARY biophysical for central-gain tinnitus).** Low-volume sustained sound stops the auditory system from amplifying gain to "find" missing input (§2.2). Modalities: **broadband / pink / brown / nature masking** (myNoise free; ReSound Relief free; Calm/Insight Timer; Oticon SoundSupport only if you already own these aids); **notch-filter** (pitch-match in TinnitusPlay/Audiogram/Mimi, play notched music 1–2 h/d — Tinnitracks is the Hannover commercial app); **fractal tones** (ReSound/Zen). **Dose 2–6 h/d at low volume just below tinnitus pitch; never combine with active noise-cancellation in silent rooms** — silence worsens central gain.

Contraindications: pregnancy, head/face malignancy, photosensitising drugs, intracranial surgery, severe seizure disorder. **Eye protection.**

**5.3 Cervical mobility + suboccipital + TMJ release (somatic tinnitus — fastest-moving lever).** Chin tucks (10×3), supine rotation, Brügger relief, doorway pec stretch, levator stretch; suboccipital tennis balls in a sock 2–5 min OR Hypervolt light setting; TMJ masseter self-massage 2 min/side + jaw "N-rest" (tongue on roof, teeth apart, lips closed); OTC bruxism night-guard (SOVA, Pro Teeth Guard); over-the-door cervical traction (Saunders/Pronex) 10–15 min 1–2×/day for confirmed cervicogenic subtype.

**5.5 Home cervical PEMF (optional).** Bemer / Healthy Wave 15 min 3×/wk. Mechanistic; small Eastern-European signal. **Contraindicated: pacemaker, pregnancy, malignancy.**

**5.6 Sauna + cold immersion (systemic).** 50–60 °C 20 min 3×/wk → cold splash 30–60 s. HSP70 + vagal + vascular. Sunlighten, HigherDOSE blanket; Plunge or bathtub + ice. **Contraindications: significant CV disease, pregnancy.**

**5.7 Hearing protection (load-bearing).** Custom-mold earplugs (Etymotic ER-20, Eargasm, Loop Engage) at concerts, power tools, gun ranges, motorcycles. **60/60 rule** for earbuds.

### 6.5 Environmental & occupational triggers to eliminate

- **Chronic noise ≥85 dB** (power tools, motorcycles, gun ranges, bars, construction) — custom-mold earplugs non-negotiable.
- **Earbud overuse** — 60/60 rule; never sleep with earbuds.
- **Ototoxic occupational chemicals** — toluene, xylene, styrene, n-hexane, heavy metals (Pb, Hg, Mn), CO; PPE; DTC urine panel if suspected.
- **Cellphone direct-to-ear chronic** — speakerphone / wired; alternate sides.
- **Endocrine disruptors** (BPA, phthalates) — glass; no plastic microwaving.
- **Mould / VOCs / formaldehyde** — ERMI / HERTSMI-2 if water-damage history; HEPA + carbon.
- **HVAC low-frequency hum** — locate and dampen.
- **Untreated sleep apnea** — home sleep study if symptomatic.
- **Untreated hearing loss** — audiogram at 35/40/45 then every 2 yr.
- **Untreated bruxism / TMJ** — OTC night-guard.
- **Light pollution + screens** — feeds insomnia → tinnitus loop.
- **Anticholinergic OTC sleep aids** (Benadryl, ZzzQuil, Unisom) — discontinue chronic.
