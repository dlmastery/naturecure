## 1. Executive Summary

PCOS is conventionally siloed as a "reproductive-endocrine" diagnosis treated with combined oral contraceptives, metformin, spironolactone, clomiphene/letrozole and (when fertility is at stake) ovarian drilling or assisted reproduction. The integrative reframing is different: PCOS is the **ovarian and dermatologic presentation of a four-node systemic network** — (i) **insulin resistance + hyperinsulinaemia** driving theca-cell androgen output, SHBG suppression and anovulation; (ii) **hyperandrogenism** (theca CYP17, 5α-reductase) producing hirsutism, acne, hair loss and follicular arrest; (iii) **chronic low-grade inflammation** (elevated hs-CRP, TNF-α, IL-6, advanced glycation end-products) amplifying ovarian dysfunction; and (iv) **gut-androgen axis dysbiosis** (depleted *Akkermansia*, expanded β-glucuronidase-producers) that recycles enterohepatic androgens. **Synthesis:** A natural, OTC, home-based protocol that simultaneously restores insulin sensitivity (inositols, berberine, ALA), lowers androgen tone (spearmint, zinc, low-glycaemic diet), dampens inflammation (omega-3, NAC, curcumin) and rebuilds the gut-androgen axis (sprouted millets, fermented foods, *Akkermansia*-supportive polyphenols) addresses the upstream drivers — not the cosmetic, menstrual or fertility endpoints in isolation.

## 2. Mechanistic Network (4-node)

**2.1 Insulin resistance + hyperinsulinaemia → ovarian theca hyperandrogenism.** Skeletal-muscle and adipocyte insulin resistance forces pancreatic β-cells to hyper-secrete insulin. Hyperinsulinaemia acts on theca-cell IGF-1R and insulin receptors, amplifying LH-driven CYP17A1 (17α-hydroxylase / 17,20-lyase) activity → testosterone, androstenedione, DHEA. Insulin also suppresses hepatic SHBG synthesis, raising free testosterone, and disrupts the LH/FSH ratio (>2:1 typical), arresting antral follicle maturation. Key actors: insulin receptor (INSR), IGF-1R, CYP17A1, AKR1C3, SHBG, AMH. Targeted by **myo-inositol + d-chiro-inositol 40:1 (Ovasitol-style), berberine, alpha-lipoic acid, magnesium glycinate, cinnamon, chromium, gymnema, low-glycaemic Khadar-Vali Siri Dhanyalu millet diet, Zone 2 cardio + strength training**. **Synthesis:** Restoring insulin sensitivity is the upstream brake on the entire cascade.

**2.2 Hyperandrogenism (clinical + biochemical) → hirsutism, acne, alopecia, anovulation.** Excess theca androgens convert peripherally via 5α-reductase to dihydrotestosterone (DHT), driving pilosebaceous unit hyperactivity (terminal hair on chin, upper lip, sideburn, chest, abdomen; sebum and acne; androgenetic scalp pattern). Intra-ovarian androgens prematurely lutein-ise granulosa cells and arrest follicular dominance → polycystic morphology + anovulatory cycles. Key actors: androgen receptor (AR), 5α-reductase types 1 and 2, AKR1C3, SHBG, AMH (chronically elevated 2–6× normal). Targeted by **spearmint tea (anti-androgen RCT-grade), zinc picolinate (5α-reductase inhibition), saw palmetto, reishi, licorice + peony classical TCM pair, Shatavari, Ashokarishta, Kanchanara Guggulu**. **Synthesis:** Lowering androgen tone reverses the cosmetic and follicular dysfunction.

**2.3 Chronic low-grade inflammation (advanced glycation, adipokine, oxidative).** PCOS cohorts show elevated hs-CRP, TNF-α, IL-6, MCP-1, advanced glycation end-products (AGEs) and oxidative stress markers (MDA, 8-OHdG). Visceral adipose tissue (often present even in lean PCOS as ectopic fat) secretes leptin, resistin, IL-6 and lowers adiponectin. AGEs cross-link ovarian follicle basement membrane, impair granulosa function and amplify androgen output. Targeted by **omega-3 (EPA+DHA 2 g), NAC, curcumin, quercetin, resveratrol, low-AGE cooking (steam/boil/poach instead of grill/fry), Mediterranean overlay, Ashwagandha, Triphala**. **Synthesis:** Without inflammation control, every other lever regresses.

**2.4 Gut-androgen axis dysbiosis + bile-acid + SCFA depletion.** PCOS cohorts show depleted *Akkermansia muciniphila*, *Bifidobacterium*, *Faecalibacterium prausnitzii*; expanded *Bacteroides*, *Escherichia/Shigella*, β-glucuronidase-producers (enterohepatic deconjugation re-cycles androgens and oestrogens back into circulation). Bile-acid pool altered (↓ secondary BAs that signal through FXR/TGR5 → metabolic dysregulation). Targeted by **multi-strain probiotic 30–50 B CFU, sprouted Siri Dhanyalu millets (foxtail, barnyard, kodo, little, browntop), fermented foods (kanji, sauerkraut, A2/coconut kefir), pomegranate + cranberry + green tea (Akkermansia-supportive polyphenols), L-glutamine, zinc-carnosine**. **Synthesis:** Sealing the gut and re-flora-ing the microbiome cuts androgen recycling and inflammation at the root.

### 6.3 Preparation methodology

**Sprouting protocol** (millets, fenugreek, mung, Bengal gram): rinse → soak filtered water 8–10 h → drain → cover in muslin → keep 25–28 °C for 12–18 h until tail-sprouts emerge → rinse twice/day.
