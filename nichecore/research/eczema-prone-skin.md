## 1. Executive Summary

Atopic eczema is conventionally siloed as an "allergic skin condition" treated with serial topical corticosteroids, calcineurin inhibitors, antihistamines and — for severe disease — dupilumab or JAK inhibitors. The integrative reframing is different: eczema is the **skin presentation of a four-node systemic loop** — (i) a structurally compromised skin barrier driven by filaggrin (FLG) loss-of-function and ceramide depletion, (ii) a Th2-skewed immune polarisation with IL-4 / IL-13 / IL-31 dominance plus mast-cell hyper-reactivity, (iii) a gut-skin axis with intestinal hyperpermeability, food-protein sensitisation, SIBO and *Bifidobacterium*/*Faecalibacterium* depletion, and (iv) HPA-axis dysregulation with sleep disruption from nocturnal pruritus driving cortisol oscillation that further erodes barrier lipids. **Synthesis:** A natural, OTC, home-based protocol that simultaneously rebuilds barrier ceramides + filaggrin precursors, modulates Th2/IL-31 itch signalling, seals and re-seeds the gut, and restores HPA-driven sleep addresses the upstream drivers — not just the visible eczematous patch.

## 2. Mechanistic Network (4-node)

**2.1 Barrier failure: filaggrin + ceramide depletion + transepidermal water loss.** Filaggrin (encoded by *FLG*) aggregates keratin filaments and breaks down to natural moisturising factor (NMF) — urocanic acid, pyrrolidone carboxylic acid — that buffer skin pH ~4.5–5.5 and retain water. Loss-of-function *FLG* variants are found in 20–50% of moderate-severe atopic eczema (highest in Northern European and Japanese cohorts). Ceramides 1, 3 and 6 are reduced; sphingomyelinase activity is altered; the stratum corneum lipid lamellae become leaky. Transepidermal water loss (TEWL) doubles. Targeted by topical ceramide-rich emollients, colloidal oat, sea buckthorn oil (omega-7 palmitoleic acid), Eladi Keram, oral omega-3, oral evening primrose / borage GLA (where evidence is mixed but mechanism supports), wet-wrap occlusion. **Synthesis:** Restoring barrier lipid and pH is the upstream brake on every downstream Th2 cascade.

**2.2 Th2 immune skew + IL-31 itch + mast-cell activation.** Antigen presentation by Langerhans cells and inflammatory dendritic epidermal cells (IDECs) drives naive CD4⁺ T-cells toward Th2 — IL-4, IL-13, IL-5, IL-31. IL-31 binds IL-31RA on cutaneous sensory neurons → itch; IL-4/IL-13 suppress filaggrin and antimicrobial peptides (LL-37, β-defensins) → barrier worsening + *Staphylococcus aureus* overgrowth (90% of moderate-severe eczema is *S. aureus* colonised). Mast-cell activation amplifies histamine, tryptase and PAF. Targeted by oral quercetin (mast-cell stabiliser), perilla seed oil (rosmarinic acid), Boswellia AKBA (5-LOX), Nigella sativa (thymoquinone TSLP modulation), curcumin (Th2 dampening), licorice topical (DGL or glycyrrhetinic acid topical), and *Lactobacillus rhamnosus* GG / *Bifidobacterium breve* M-16V probiotics (Th1/Treg rebalancing — EMA-recognised strains). **Synthesis:** Damping Th2/IL-31 reduces both itch and barrier-erosive cytokines simultaneously.

**2.3 Gut-skin axis: leaky gut + food-protein sensitisation + SIBO + microbiome depletion.** Eczema cohorts show elevated zonulin, low *Bifidobacterium*, low *Faecalibacterium prausnitzii*, expanded *Clostridium difficile* / *Enterobacteriaceae*. Early-life dysbiosis predicts atopic march (eczema → food allergy → allergic rhinitis → asthma). Food-protein sensitisation (egg, milk, peanut, soy, wheat) and SIBO drive systemic histamine and Th2 priming via mesenteric lymph nodes. Maternal/infant microbiome shaping (vaginal delivery, breastfeeding, early diverse-food exposure per LEAP trial principles) is upstream. Targeted by gluten + ultra-processed elimination, L-glutamine, zinc-carnosine, DGL licorice, slippery elm, marshmallow root, fermented foods, multi-strain probiotic with *L. rhamnosus* GG + *B. breve* M-16V + *L. paracasei* CNCM I-2116 (EMA pediatric atopic-eczema strains), and pomegranate / cranberry / *Akkermansia*-supportive polyphenols. **Synthesis:** No topical layer holds if the gut barrier and microbial reservoir keep priming Th2.

**2.4 HPA-axis + nocturnal pruritus + sleep disruption + autonomic dysregulation.** Eczema disrupts sleep in 60–80% of patients via nocturnal itch; sleep restriction in turn raises evening cortisol, lowers DHEA, shifts the cortisol-awakening response and erodes ceramide synthesis (cortisol suppresses keratinocyte lipid biosynthesis). Stress is the single most cited acute flare trigger across European, Indian and Japanese cohorts. Vagal tone (HRV-rMSSD) is reduced in moderate-severe eczema. Targeted by Ashwagandha KSM-66, *Bacopa monnieri*, L-theanine, magnesium glycinate, melatonin (≤0.3 mg physiological dose), pranayama (Anulom Vilom, Bhramari, Sheetali), Yoga Nidra, sleep hygiene, vagal practices (humming, gargling, cold splash), and consumer VNS (Nurosym, Sensate). **Synthesis:** Without HPA + sleep repair, every other lever regresses inside the next stress cycle.

Dose by Fitzpatrick type: I 5–10 min, II 10–15, III 15–20, IV 20–25, V 30, VI 30–40 min. Windows **09:00–11:00 AM** and **15:30–17:00 PM**. Avoid summer solar noon. **NOT the same as clinic NB-UVB cabin — this is a home administered vitamin D + circadian + immunomodulatory anchor.** Acute weeping flare: skip until settled.

**5.3 Wet-wrap occlusion (zero-cost biophysical, eczema mainstay).** Bedtime: gentle bath (lukewarm 32–35 °C, 10 min, colloidal-oat or Dead Sea bath salts 250 g), pat dry leaving slight dampness, apply ceramide emollient or **Eladi Keram** (Kottakkal) + **Mahanarayan Taila** to plaques, occlude with damp cotton tubular bandages (Tubifast) under dry layer overnight or 1–2 h sessions. **SCORAD often drops 30–50% within 7–10 nights of consistent wet-wrap.** This is the highest-yield zero-cost intervention in eczema. Contraindications: infected skin (treat infection first), open weeping with crust (gentle compress only).

**5.4 Home infrared sauna** (Sunlighten, JNH, Clearlight) — 45–55 °C, 15–20 min, 2×/week. **CAUTION: heat aggravates acute eczema; only use in stable/chronic dry phenotype, never during weeping flare.** HSP70 induction, sweat-mediated phenolic / BPA elimination. Always cool rinse + re-moisturise within 3 min.

**5.5 Home cold-water immersion / contrast hydrotherapy.** Cold splash AM (face + forearms); brief cold shower 30–60 s end of warm shower. Vagal afferent + norepinephrine + anti-pruritic. **Full cold immersion (10–15 °C tub) only in stable chronic phenotype**; avoid during acute flare (cold-induced histamine release in some patients).

**5.6 Dead Sea bath salts (home alternative).** Magnesium chloride + Bromide + Potassium — **250–500 g per bath, 20 min, 2–3×/week**. **5.7 Colloidal oatmeal bath** — 1 cup colloidal oat (Aveeno generic, Avène, Eucerin) in lukewarm bath, 10–15 min. Soothes pruritus via avenanthramides + β-glucans.

**5.8 Home humidifier in winter** — 40–55% indoor humidity. Below 30% RH, TEWL doubles in barrier-compromised skin. Cool-mist ultrasonic (Levoit, Dyson) preferred.

### 6.3 Preparation methodology

**Sprouting protocol** (millets, mung, Bengal gram): rinse → soak filtered water 8–10 h → drain → muslin cover → 25–28 °C for 12–18 h until tail-sprouts. Reduces phytic acid 30–50%, raises GABA + B-vitamins.

**Bone broth** (gut-sealing): grass-fed marrow + knuckle bones + onion + garlic + carrot + apple cider vinegar 2 tbsp + filtered water → simmer 12–24 h → strain → refrigerate; consume warm 200–300 ml AM and / or post-workout.

**Ghee tempering** of bitter greens with cumin + turmeric — enhances fat-soluble vitamin + curcumin absorption.

**Tea brewing — chamomile (HMPC monograph):** 1 tbsp dried flowers in 250 ml just-off-boil water, steep 5 min covered, 2 cups PM.

**Eladi Keram warm-application:** decant 30 ml into a small ceramic bowl, warm to 35–38 °C, massage gently into plaques, leave 60 min, wet-wrap occlude for highest yield.

### 6.5 Environmental & occupational triggers to eliminate

- **SLS / SLES cleansers, fragranced shower gel, antibacterial soaps** — strip barrier lipids, raise skin pH → ↓ filaggrin, ↑ TEWL. Switch to **syndet pH 5.5 cleansers** (La Roche-Posay Lipikar Syndet, Cetaphil Pro Restoraderm, Avène Trixera, Bioderma Atoderm, Eucerin AtoControl).
- **Fragrance / parfum / botanical EOs in leave-on products** — type-IV contact allergy; eliminate or patch-test. Limonene, linalool, hydroxycitronellal, cinnamal are top offenders.
- **Lanolin, propylene glycol, methylisothiazolinone (MI/MCI) preservatives** — common contact allergens; check ingredient lists.
- **Nickel + cobalt** (jewellery, belt buckles, jeans-stud, mobile phone backplate, coins) — dyshidrotic eczema trigger; switch to titanium / surgical-grade / coated jewellery. Low-nickel diet (Tel Aviv cohort) reduces dyshidrotic flares.
- **Wool / synthetic rough fabric** next to skin — switch to **100% cotton, bamboo, silk** (silk significantly reduces SCORAD per Italian RCT — DermaSilk, AlphaSilk).
- **Hard water (high Ca, Mg, Cl)** — UK cohort (Childhood Eczema in the Living Environment) links hard water to flare. Install whole-house softener or shower-head filter (Hello Klean, Jolie).
- **Indoor allergens** — dust mite, mould, cockroach, pet dander. Allergen-proof mattress + pillow covers (Mission Allergy, AllergyCare); HEPA filter (Dyson, Levoit, IQAir); weekly hot-wash bed linen ≥60 °C.
- **Cigarette smoke / wood smoke / VOCs** — IL-31 and TSLP amplifier; eliminate exposure.
- **Chlorinated pool water** — rinse + emollient within 3 min of exit; consider salt-water pool or ocean instead.
- **Fragranced laundry detergents + dryer sheets** — switch to fragrance-free liquid (Persil Sensitive, Ecover, Surcare); double-rinse cycle.
- **BPA / phthalates / endocrine disruptors** — glass food containers, no microwave plastics, fragrance-free personal care.
- **Hand sanitiser + frequent hand-wash without re-emollient** — occupational hand eczema (healthcare, food service, hairdresser). Re-moisturise after every hand-wash with ceramide-rich balm.

### 10.5 Exercise / Sleep / Caffeine timing rules

- **Caffeine cut-off 14:00** — half-life 6 h; protects sleep + cortisol; nocturnal pruritus worsens with caffeine residue.
- **Alcohol** — eliminate during active flare (histamine, sleep disruption, Zn/B-vit depletion, hepatic CYP load).
- **Ashwagandha KSM-66** — PM with dinner; ≥2 h before bed; if vivid dreams, shift AM.
- **Magnesium glycinate** — PM (sleep + bowel motility); split from Ca which goes AM.
- **L-theanine + caffeine** — AM only.
- **Melatonin** — 0.3–1 mg, 30–60 min before sleep; safe in childhood eczema per Italian RCT.
- **Probiotic** — cool first-meal or empty stomach AM; ≥2 h from any antibiotic.
- **Inverted yoga** (Sarvangasana, Halasana) — before breakfast only; never after dinner; never with reflux; **skip during acute flare**.
- **Sauna + alcohol** — never combine.
- **Sweat-aware exercise** — pre-emollient before sweat; cool rinse + re-emollient within 3 min.
- **Pool chlorine** — rinse + emollient within 3 min; salt-water pool or ocean preferred.
- **Hot showers** — limit to 5 min ≤38 °C; lukewarm 32–35 °C is barrier-friendlier.
- **PBM** — eye shield mandatory; skip on acute weeping; ≥2 h from final topical active.
- **Don't scratch** — use habit-reversal (notice → pause 30 s → competing response of cool press / emollient).

### 10.6 Strictly Avoid — specific to eczema pathology

- **St John's Wort** — CYP3A4 / Pgp induction (interferes with cyclosporine, dupilumab metabolism less, JAK inhibitors); serotonin issues if on SSRIs; photosensitisation.
- **High-dose Echinacea / Astragalus** — autoimmune-thyroid overlap; counter-action with biologic immunomodulators.
- **High-dose vitamin A** — pregnancy, isotretinoin if on it.
- **Real licorice (glycyrrhizin)** — pseudohyperaldosteronism, BP rise, hypokalemia; use DGL only.
- **Fragranced botanical EOs in leave-on products** — contact allergy.
- **Lanolin, MI/MCI preservatives, propylene glycol** in topicals — contact allergens.
- **Wool next to skin, nickel jewellery** — contact triggers.
- **SLS / SLES cleansers** — barrier strippers.
- **Chlorinated pool without immediate rinse + emollient.**
- **Hot baths >40 °C, hot saunas during flare.**
- **Kapalbhati / Bhastrika** in HTN, pregnancy, ulcer, glaucoma.
- **Smoking, vaping** — barrier impairment + oxidative load.

## 11. Clinical Scoring + Comorbidity Screening

### 11.0 Self-administered clinical scoring instrument — SCORAD + POEM + DLQI

**SCORAD (SCORing Atopic Dermatitis)** — combines BSA (rule-of-nines, palm = 1%), intensity (erythema, oedema, oozing, excoriation, lichenification, dryness — each 0–3) and subjective symptoms (pruritus VAS 0–10 + sleep-loss VAS 0–10). Total 0–103. Self-administrable with smartphone camera + standardised photo protocol.

**POEM (Patient-Oriented Eczema Measure)** — 7 items × 0–4 weekly; total 0–28; gold-standard patient-reported outcome.

**Procedure** (every 2 weeks for POEM; every 4 weeks for SCORAD): standardise lighting (5000 K LED or north window at 08:00); distance (30 cm close-ups, 1 m whole-body); 8 fixed views (face front, neck, chest, back, both antecubital fossae, both popliteal fossae, hands, feet); mid-grey background; same time of day; same emollient status (always before AM emollient or 2 h after). Save JPEG + RAW to encrypted cloud (Proton Drive, iCloud E2E). Apps: **Eczema Tracker (UK)**, **AtopicHealth**, **SkinAI**.

**Cadence:** baseline (Wk 0), POEM every 2 wk, SCORAD Wk 4 / 8 / 12 / Mo 6 / Mo 9 / Mo 12.

**Adjunct self-scoring:** **DLQI** (Dermatology Life Quality Index, 10 items) baseline + Mo3/6/12; **PSQI** (Pittsburgh Sleep Quality Index) — eczema's sleep disruption is the #1 quality-of-life destroyer; **PHQ-9 + GAD-7** quarterly (eczema ↑ depression + anxiety ~2×); **PGI-C** at each window.

## 13.5 Patient-Facing Primer

Eczema is not "just dry skin". Inside your body, four things are happening together: (1) the outer barrier of your skin is structurally leaky — a protein called filaggrin and the natural fats called ceramides are low, so water escapes and irritants get in; (2) your immune system is tilted toward an allergic-type response (Th2) that releases an itch-signal called IL-31 and recruits mast cells; (3) the lining of your gut is also leaky in many people, food-protein fragments are getting through, and friendly bacteria like *Bifidobacterium* are depleted; and (4) the itch destroys your sleep, your stress hormone cortisol oscillates wildly, and that further erodes your barrier lipids — a vicious circle.

This protocol does **not** chase the rash with months of stronger and stronger steroids or jump straight to biologics. Instead, it rebuilds each of the four layers — with ceramide-rich emollients, classical Ayurvedic oils like Eladi Keram and Mahanarayan, wet-wrap occlusion, omega-3 + vitamin D + zinc, mast-cell calmers like quercetin and Nigella, gut-sealing bone broth + L-glutamine + probiotic strains *Lactobacillus rhamnosus* GG and *Bifidobacterium breve* M-16V (the EMA-recognised paediatric atopic strains), and sleep-restoration tools (Yoga Nidra, melatonin micro-dose, cool dark cotton bed). **No topical-steroid escalation. No oral cyclosporine or methotrexate. No biologic infusion. No clinic phototherapy. No hospital procedure.**

Expect a slow, steady re-balancing. The first thing you will notice (Weeks 1–3) is the itch easing and sleep returning. Around Week 12 the lichenified plaques start to soften and skin texture rebuilds. Real long-term remission is a 6-to-12-month story, especially if you also have hand eczema or dyshidrotic patterns. You are sovereign over your own body. If you choose to keep a dermatologist informed, that is your personal decision. The protocol does not require it.
