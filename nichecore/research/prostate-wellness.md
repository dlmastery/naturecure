## 1. Executive Summary

Prostate disease is conventionally siloed as "BPH treated with α-blockers + 5-ARIs + (eventually) surgery", "chronic prostatitis treated with antibiotics + α-blockers + anti-cholinergics", or "prostate cancer screened by PSA + DRE → biopsy → treat". The integrative reframing is different: prostate health across BPH, CPPS and primary prevention is the **signature of a four-node network** — (i) **androgen-to-DHT pathway dysregulation** (5α-reductase converts T → DHT, the prostatic growth signal; DHT excess + AR overexpression drives BPH stromal-epithelial hyperplasia); (ii) **chronic prostatic inflammation** (Th17, NF-κB, PSA expression, prostatic intraepithelial neoplasia precursors, infection, EDC exposure); (iii) **pelvic-floor dysfunction + autonomic dysregulation** (driving CPPS pain and urinary symptoms via sympathetic overdrive, vagal underdrive, hypertonic pelvic floor); and (iv) **metabolic-vascular-cardiovascular co-burden** (metabolic syndrome + insulin resistance + NAFLD + visceral adiposity feed prostate inflammation; ED is co-prevalent and shares vascular mechanism). **Synthesis:** A natural, OTC, home-based protocol that simultaneously moderates 5α-reductase + DHT (saw palmetto EMA, beta-sitosterol, pumpkin seed oil, stinging nettle root, zinc), dampens prostate inflammation (lycopene, omega-3, curcumin + boswellia, green tea EGCG, pomegranate), restores pelvic-floor function (yoga, biofeedback, paradoxical-relaxation, perineal massage), and clears metabolic-vascular-EDC burden (Mediterranean overlay, strength + Zone 2, sauna, organic diet, BPA removal) addresses the upstream drivers — for both BPH and CPPS, while supporting prevention.

## 2. Mechanistic Network (4-node)

**2.1 Androgen-to-DHT pathway + AR over-signalling.** Testosterone is converted to 5α-dihydrotestosterone (DHT) by 5α-reductase (types 1, 2, 3). Type 2 predominates in prostatic stroma. DHT is 3× more potent at androgen receptor (AR) than T. Stromal-epithelial paracrine signalling drives hyperplastic growth. Key actors: SRD5A1, SRD5A2, AR, 17β-HSD, PSA (KLK3), KLK2, INSL3. Targeted by **Saw palmetto (*Serenoa repens*, EMA HMPC), Beta-sitosterol, Pumpkin seed oil (*Cucurbita pepo*, EMA HMPC), Stinging nettle root (*Urtica dioica*, EMA HMPC), Zinc, Pygeum (*Prunus africana*) EMA HMPC, Rye-grass pollen extract (Cernilton)**. **Synthesis:** Modulating 5α-reductase + DHT lowers prostatic growth signal — without finasteride's sexual side effects.

**2.2 Chronic prostatic inflammation + immune dysregulation.** Histological prostatitis is found in 50–70% of BPH specimens. Th17/IL-17, NF-κB activation, TNF-α, IL-6, IL-8 drive prostatic inflammation. Trigger sources: subclinical infection (uropathogenic *E. coli*, *Mycoplasma*, *Chlamydia*; Category II prostatitis), EDC exposure (BPA, phthalates), high-glycaemic diet + AGE, smoking, metabolic syndrome. Inflammation amplifies AR signalling and prostatic intraepithelial neoplasia precursor risk. Targeted by **Lycopene 10–15 mg, Omega-3 EPA+DHA 2 g, Curcumin + Boswellia, Green tea EGCG, Pomegranate, Quercetin (specific RCT for CPPS), Vitamin D + K2, Multi-strain probiotic, gut + vaginal/penile microbiome work**. **Synthesis:** Without inflammation control, growth and pain regress.

**2.3 Pelvic-floor dysfunction + autonomic dysregulation (CPPS-dominant).** Chronic prostatitis Category III (CPPS) is non-bacterial — pain, urgency, frequency, dysuria, perineal/penile/testicular discomfort driven by **pelvic-floor hypertonicity + sympathetic overdrive + vagal underdrive + central sensitisation**. Stanford / UPOINT framework (Shoskes). Tight pelvic floor compresses neurovascular bundle → pain + voiding dysfunction + ED. Targeted by **pelvic-floor relaxation (paradoxical relaxation, Wise-Anderson protocol), trigger-point release (perineal self-massage, Theracane), pelvic-floor yoga (hip openers, restorative), biofeedback (Elvie, Perifit, Pelvic-floor PT app), Magnesium glycinate for muscle tone, Ashwagandha + Bacopa for HPA, MBSR, paradoxical-relaxation training, cold immersion vagal**. **Synthesis:** CPPS will not resolve without pelvic-floor + autonomic re-training — supplements alone are insufficient.

**2.4 Metabolic-vascular-EDC co-burden.** Metabolic syndrome + insulin resistance + visceral adiposity + NAFLD double BPH progression risk. Visceral adipose aromatase converts T → oestradiol → SHBG → low free T (paradoxical — low T contributes to BPH via altered T:E ratio at AR). EDC exposure (BPA, phthalates, atrazine, PFAS) directly affects prostate. Targeted by **Berberine, Milk thistle, Picrorhiza, Mediterranean overlay, strength + Zone 2, infrared sauna, organic diet, BPA elimination**. **Synthesis:** Without metabolic-EDC repair, prostate symptoms recur.
