It is **not** IVF, not IUI, not ovulation-induction drugs, and not a fertility-clinic protocol. It is the layered, honest, evidence-graded preconception path: 90 days of egg-and-sperm-quality work (that's how long both gametes take to mature), folate-iron-D3-omega-3 substrate floor, methylation support, environmental clean-up (BPA out, alcohol out, smoking zero, heat-to-testicles out), CoQ10 for both partners, L-carnitine for him, myo-inositol for her, the right cycle-tracking tools, and the honest "if we've tried for 12 months (6 months if 35+) without success, here's the clinic-route map." *(WHO TCIM Strategy 2025-2034; ESHRE preconception guideline 2024; Cochrane CoQ10 fertility 2020)*

> **Red flags (rule-out, not self-manage).** (1) Trying ≥12 months under 35, or ≥6 months at 35+, without conception — formal fertility workup is appropriate. ## 1. Executive Summary

### 1.1 What "fertility readiness" actually is

> **For you, in plain words.** Fertility is rarely one thing — it is the integrated readiness of egg, sperm, uterus, cycle, environment and lifestyle. Naming all six lets every food, supplement and habit aim at the right one. *(ESHRE preconception 2024; Lefebvre 2020 fertility cohort)*

### 1.2 The six causes — at a glance

> **Quick start.** **BOTH partners** tonight: alcohol cut to zero or near-zero, smoking zero, lights-out 22:30, phone outside bedroom. **BOTH this week**: prenatal multi (methylfolate 800 µg + methyl-B12 + iron if low + D3 4000 IU + choline) + CoQ10 200-400 mg + omega-3 EPA+DHA 2 g + magnesium 300 mg PM + sauna-and-cold for him (he stops if sperm count low — heat hurts) / for her (no contraindication). **HE adds**: L-carnitine 2 g + zinc 15 mg + selenium 200 µg + Ashwagandha 600 mg PM. **SHE adds**: Myo-inositol 2 g BID + Shatavari 500 mg BID + Vitex 175-225 mg AM (ONLY if luteal-phase short; STOP at confirmed conception). **THIS MONTH**: order DTC AMH (her) + DTC semen analysis (him) + cycle tracker (Mira / Inito / Tempdrop) + full nutrient panel both. *(Bentov 2014 CoQ10 oocyte; Lafuente 2013 CoQ10 sperm; Lenzi 2003 L-carnitine sperm; Unfer 2014 myo-inositol)*

### 1.3 The deeper layer — for those who want it

Fertility is conventionally siloed as "infertility" managed with timed-intercourse counselling → clomiphene/letrozole ovulation induction → IUI → IVF/ICSI → donor gametes → surrogacy. The integrative reframing is different: fertility is the **multi-system signature of a six-node network** — (i) **female egg quality and ovarian reserve** (AMH, antral follicle count, mitochondrial function in oocytes); (ii) **female ovulation regularity and luteal-phase competence** (hypothalamic-pituitary-ovarian axis, FSH-LH-progesterone signalling); (iii) **male sperm parameters** (count, motility, morphology, DNA fragmentation index); (iv) **uterine receptivity** (endometrial thickness and quality, immune tolerance, microbiome); (v) **EDC body burden + sleep + nutrient floor + metabolic-thyroid status** (BPA, phthalates impair both gametes; sleep loss drops T and progesterone; sub-clinical zinc, selenium, vitamin D, folate, choline deficiencies are widespread); (vi) **psychosocial-stress load + couple timing-and-frequency education** (HPA → ovulation suppression; correct fertile-window targeting). **Synthesis.** A home protocol that simultaneously supports both gametes' 90-day maturation cycle, restores ovulation regularity, optimises uterine receptivity, clears EDC burden, fills the nutrient floor, supports HPA, and educates timing — gives the majority of couples a 60-80% conception rate within 6-12 months without IVF.

## 2. Mechanistic Network (6-node)

### 2.1 Six causes — the picture

> **For you, in plain words.** Six drivers explain almost every fertility delay — egg quality, ovulation regularity, sperm parameters, uterine readiness, EDC-and-nutrient floor, and timing-and-stress. *(ESHRE 2024; Lefebvre 2020)*

### 2.2 Egg quality + ovarian reserve

> **For you, in plain words.** Eggs take 90 days to mature for ovulation — the 90-day window before each cycle is when you can influence egg quality. CoQ10, melatonin, myo-inositol and NAC are the mitochondrial cornerstones. *(Bentov 2014 CoQ10 oocyte; Tamura 2008 melatonin IVF)*

**Quick start.** CoQ10 ubiquinol 200-400 mg/day + melatonin 3 mg HS (preconception only — stop at confirmed conception) + myo-inositol 2 g BID + NAC 600 mg BID + sleep optimisation.

#### Open the science

Oocyte mitochondria are critical for meiotic spindle assembly, chromosome segregation, fertilisation energy, and early embryo development. With age, mitochondrial dysfunction rises, aneuploidy increases, and AMH (anti-Müllerian hormone — a proxy for antral follicle pool) drops. **Bentov 2014** (Canadian RCT, n=39) showed CoQ10 600 mg/day for 60 days reduced aneuploidy in older women's oocytes. **Tamura 2008** (Japanese clinical trial) showed melatonin 3 mg/d for women undergoing IVF improved oocyte quality and pregnancy rate. Key actors: mitochondrial complex I-IV, ATP production, oxidative stress markers, SIRT3, AMH. Targeted by **CoQ10 ubiquinol (preferred over ubiquinone in older women), melatonin (3 mg HS preconception only), myo-inositol, NAC, alpha-lipoic acid, vitamin E, vitamin C, zinc, selenium, omega-3, sleep optimisation, low-AGE cooking**. **Synthesis.** The 90-day pre-conception window is the highest-leverage time for egg quality.

### 2.3 Ovulation regularity + luteal phase

> **For you, in plain words.** A regular ovulatory cycle with a 12-14 day luteal phase is the foundation. Vitex (chasteberry) supports luteal phase; Shatavari supports the female reproductive system; B6 supports progesterone synthesis. *(Schellenberg 2001 Vitex BMJ RCT; Indian Shatavari PCOS trials)*

**Quick start.** Cycle tracking (Mira / Inito / Tempdrop / Oura) from day 1 + Vitex 175-225 mg AM (ONLY if luteal-phase short on three consecutive cycles) + Shatavari 500 mg BID + B6 (P-5-P) 50 mg + zinc 15 mg PM.

#### Open the science

Hypothalamic GnRH pulsatility (~every 90 min) drives anterior pituitary FSH (follicular phase) and LH (preovulatory surge) which trigger ovulation; the corpus luteum then secretes progesterone for ~14 days (luteal phase). A short luteal phase (<10 days) or low mid-luteal progesterone (<10 ng/mL) signals luteal insufficiency. **Schellenberg 2001** (German RCT, n=170, BMJ) showed Vitex agnus-castus reduces PMS and supports cycle regularity. Key actors: GnRH, FSH, LH, oestradiol, progesterone, prolactin, leptin, kisspeptin. Targeted by **Vitex (luteal support; D2-receptor → prolactin moderation), Shatavari, B6 (P-5-P) (progesterone cofactor), zinc, magnesium, Yoga Nidra, HPA-axis work, low-stress lifestyle**. **Synthesis.** Without ovulation and adequate luteal phase, conception cannot occur.

### 2.4 Sperm parameters + DNA quality

> **For you, in plain words.** Sperm take 90 days to mature in the testicle plus another 14 in the epididymis. The 90-day pre-conception window decides count, motility, morphology and DNA quality. L-carnitine + CoQ10 + zinc + selenium + omega-3 are the cornerstones. *(Lenzi 2003 L-carnitine RCT; Lafuente 2013 CoQ10 sperm meta-analysis; WHO 2021 semen analysis)*

**Quick start.** L-carnitine + acetyl-L-carnitine 2 g + 500 mg + CoQ10 200-400 mg + zinc 15 mg PM + selenium 100-200 µg (2 Brazil nuts) + omega-3 EPA+DHA 2 g + vitamin D3 4000 IU AM + Ashwagandha KSM-66 600 mg PM + abstain from heat to testicles + alcohol zero.

#### Open the science

Spermatogenesis is a 74-day process in the testis plus 14 days in the epididymis. Key parameters per WHO 2021: count ≥16 M/mL, motility ≥30% progressive, morphology ≥4% normal forms, DNA Fragmentation Index <30%. Sperm are particularly vulnerable to oxidative stress (high PUFA content in membrane). **Lenzi 2003** (Italian RCT, n=86, Fertil Steril) showed L-carnitine 2 g/day for 4 months improved sperm motility. **Lafuente 2013** (Spanish meta-analysis) showed CoQ10 improves sperm count and motility. Key actors: ROS, mitochondrial function in sperm tail, DNA fragmentation, protamine, oxidative stress markers. Targeted by **L-carnitine + ALCAR, CoQ10 ubiquinol, zinc, selenium, vitamin E, vitamin C, omega-3 DHA, Ashwagandha (Ambiye 2013 sperm RCT), Mucuna pruriens (Shukla 2010), Shilajit (Biswas 2010), folate (sperm DNA methylation), B12, sauna AVOIDANCE if oligospermia, heat-to-testicles avoidance, alcohol cessation**. **Synthesis.** The 90-day pre-conception window is the highest-leverage time for sperm quality.

### 2.5 Uterine receptivity

> **For you, in plain words.** Even with perfect egg and sperm, the endometrium has to be receptive at the right time — Day 19-23 of a 28-day cycle ("implantation window"). Endometrial thickness ≥7-8 mm, healthy uterine microbiome, low inflammation. *(ESHRE endometrium guideline; Moreno 2016 endometrial microbiome)*

**Quick start.** Anti-inflammatory diet + L-arginine 3 g AM (endometrial blood flow) + vitamin E 200 IU + omega-3 + treat any vaginal/uterine dysbiosis (lactobacillus-dominant via fermented foods + oral probiotic), Femina app for cycle window.

#### Open the science

The endometrium thickens under oestradiol in the follicular phase, decidualises under progesterone in the luteal phase, and is receptive (implantation window) cycle days 19-23. Adequate uterine blood flow (Doppler PI <3.0), endometrial thickness ≥7-8 mm, lactobacillus-dominant uterine microbiome (>90% lactobacilli, per Moreno 2016 Spanish endometrial microbiome study), and absence of chronic endometritis predict implantation success. Key actors: integrins (αvβ3), LIF, HOXA10, endometrial NK cells, decidualisation markers. Targeted by **anti-inflammatory diet, L-arginine, vitamin E, vitamin C, omega-3 EPA+DHA, oral + vaginal lactobacillus probiotics, vitamin D, Mediterranean-millet base, avoidance of cycle disruptors**. **Synthesis.** Without uterine receptivity, no implantation — even with euploid embryo.

### 2.6 EDC + sleep + nutrient floor + thyroid

> **For you, in plain words.** Plastics, pesticides, late-night screens, missed sleep, silent zinc/selenium/folate/D3 deficiency, and undiagnosed subclinical hypothyroidism (TSH >2.5 IU/mL = preconception action zone) all directly drop fertility for both partners. *(Hauser 2015 BPA-semen; Negro 2007 subclinical hypothyroid + fertility; ICMR folate)*

**Quick start.** Glass storage + organic dirty-dozen + Berkey water + lights-out 22:30 + DTC thyroid panel both partners + cofactor floor met daily.

#### Open the science

Endocrine disruptors (BPA, phthalates, atrazine, glyphosate, organochlorine pesticides, PFAS, heavy metals lead/cadmium) directly impair both ovarian and testicular function. Sleep loss drops both female progesterone and male testosterone. Sub-clinical hypothyroidism (TSH >2.5 mIU/L preconception) significantly raises miscarriage and reduces fertility (**Negro 2007** Italian RCT). Targeted by **glass storage, organic produce, clean cosmetics, PFAS-free cookware, water filter, infrared sauna for sweat-driven EDC clearance, full cofactor floor (§6.7), TSH treated to <2.5 mIU/L preconception (with iodine + selenium support; thyroid hormone Rx if clinical hypothyroidism — outside this protocol)**. **Synthesis.** Without burden reduction + sleep + nutrient floor + thyroid optimisation, the other layers regress.

### 2.7 Stress + timing-and-frequency education

> **For you, in plain words.** Chronic stress suppresses ovulation through hypothalamic GnRH; many couples conceive faster simply by switching from "every other day" to "every day in the fertile window" or by correctly identifying the fertile window. *(Wischmann 2009 stress-infertility review; Stanford 2002 fertility-window education)*

**Quick start.** Timed intercourse — cycle day 10 onwards every 1-2 days through day 18 (or per cycle tracker fertile window), Ashwagandha for both, sex-positive low-shame couples conversation, MBSR or yoga for HPA.

#### Open the science

Chronic stress suppresses hypothalamic GnRH pulsatility through CRH and cortisol. Misidentification of the fertile window is one of the most common reasons couples present with "subfertility" who in fact have correct gametes and ovulation. **Stanford 2002** (Fertil Steril) showed correct fertile-window education shortens time-to-conception. Key actors: CRH, cortisol, hypothalamic GnRH, kisspeptin, prolactin (stress raises). Targeted by **Ashwagandha (both partners), MBSR, Yoga Nidra, sex-positive couples therapy, cycle tracking (Mira / Inito / Tempdrop / Oura), sperm-friendly lubricants (Pre-Seed) instead of spermicidal**. **Synthesis.** Timing + stress reduction may be the simplest unaddressed lever.

## 4. Traditional Medicine Integration

### 4.1 Six traditions — at a glance

## 13.5 Patient-Facing Primer

### Read this first if you read nothing else

> **For you, in plain words.** Fertility is rarely one thing — it is the integrated readiness of egg, sperm, uterus, cycle, environment and lifestyle. This protocol rebuilds each layer at home, both partners, over the 90 days both gametes need to mature. *(Bentov 2014; Lenzi 2003; ESHRE 2024)*

Fertility is not just "trying." Expect a 6-to-12-month story. The 90-day pre-conception window (weeks 5-13) is the highest-leverage time — both gametes mature during it. If you have been trying for 12 months under 35 (or 6 months at 35+) without success, formal fertility workup is appropriate — the protocol does not replace it; it complements it. You are sovereign over your own bodies.
