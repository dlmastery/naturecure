## 1. Executive Summary

Subfertility is conventionally siloed into "infertility" diagnoses treated with ovulation induction (clomiphene, letrozole), gonadotropin injections, intrauterine insemination (IUI), in-vitro fertilisation (IVF) and intracytoplasmic sperm injection (ICSI). The integrative reframing is different: fertility readiness is the **multi-system optimisation of a four-node network across BOTH partners** — (i) **gametic quality** (oocyte mitochondrial function, sperm count + motility + morphology + DNA integrity); (ii) **endocrine signalling** (HPO/HPT axis, luteal phase progesterone, sperm-supporting testosterone, thyroid sufficiency); (iii) **endometrial receptivity + implantation environment** (uterine blood flow, lining quality, immune tolerance, Th17/Treg balance); and (iv) **systemic resilience + EDC-detox + nutrient repletion** (oxidative stress, methylation, micronutrient floor, endocrine-disruptor body burden). **Synthesis:** A natural, OTC, home-based protocol that simultaneously upgrades gametic quality (CoQ10, omega-3 DHA, NAC, methylfolate, B12, vitamin D, selenium, zinc; for men: maca, fenugreek, ashwagandha, L-carnitine, vitamin E), supports endocrine signalling (Shatavari, Vitex with caution, Ashwagandha, Liu Wei Di Huang Wan + Wen Jing Tang), reinforces endometrial receptivity (omega-3, L-arginine, vitamin E, uterine-warming TCM) and clears systemic burden (sprouted millets, EDC removal, sauna detox, gut work) creates a fertile window 3–6 months before conception attempts — for both partners.

## 2. Mechanistic Network (4-node, both partners)

**2.1 Gametic quality — oocyte mitochondria + sperm parameters + DNA integrity.** Oocyte mitochondrial DNA copy number (typically 100,000–600,000) directly predicts fertilisation, blastocyst formation and implantation; mitochondrial quality declines with age, oxidative stress, EDC exposure and metabolic burden. Sperm count (>15 M/mL WHO 2021), progressive motility (>32%), normal morphology (>4% Kruger strict) and **DNA fragmentation index (SDFI <15% excellent, 15–30% borderline, >30% poor)** are the four pillars; SDFI is most predictive of miscarriage and IVF failure but rarely measured. Key actors: ATP synthase complex V, oocyte SIRT1/SIRT3, mtDNA copy number, NAD+, protamine 1/2 (sperm), MnSOD, GPX4, telomere length. Targeted by **CoQ10 (ubiquinol) 200–600 mg, NAC 600 mg BID, omega-3 DHA, vitamin E (mixed gamma-tocopherol), selenium 100–200 µg, methylfolate, methyl-B12, L-carnitine + acetyl-L-carnitine for sperm motility, zinc, lycopene, astaxanthin, R-ALA, sperm-specific: maca, fenugreek, ashwagandha, tribulus (caution), pine bark extract**. **Synthesis:** Gametes are made 90 days in advance — every conception cycle reflects supplement+lifestyle quality from 3 months prior.

**2.2 Endocrine signalling — HPO + HPT + adrenal + thyroid.** Female: hypothalamic GnRH pulsatility → pituitary LH/FSH → ovarian follicular maturation + oestradiol surge → LH ovulation peak → luteal corpus luteum progesterone (8–22 ng/mL day 21 of 28-day cycle, ideally >15). Insufficient luteal progesterone (luteal phase defect, <10 ng/mL) → implantation failure. Male: hypothalamic GnRH → LH + FSH → Leydig cell testosterone + Sertoli cell sperm maturation. Both: thyroid (TSH <2.5 mIU/L ideal preconception; >2.5 associated with subfertility and miscarriage), adrenal cortisol-DHEA balance, prolactin (high prolactin suppresses GnRH). Targeted by **Vitex agnus-castus (caution — see §10), Shatavari, Ashwagandha, Liu Wei Di Huang Wan + Wen Jing Tang (TCM uterine warming), Maca, Tribulus (male, caution), B6 (P-5-P) for luteal phase, vitamin D, iodine (cautious in autoimmune-thyroid), selenium**. **Synthesis:** Without endocrine signalling correction, gametes never reach the rendezvous.

**2.3 Endometrial receptivity + implantation environment.** Day 19–24 "window of implantation" requires adequate endometrial thickness (≥8 mm trilaminar pattern), good uterine artery perfusion, balanced Th17/Treg immunity, low chronic inflammation, no Asherman's, no endometritis. Recurrent pregnancy loss (RPL) often involves autoimmune (anti-cardiolipin, lupus anticoagulant, ANA, anti-thyroid in some), hypercoagulable (factor V Leiden, MTHFR, prothrombin), endometrial chronic inflammation, or balanced translocation. Targeted by **L-arginine, vitamin E, omega-3, low-AGE diet, uterine-warming TCM (Wen Jing Tang, You Gui Wan), pelvic warmth (avoid prolonged cold immersion in luteal phase), pelvic-floor + uterine yoga, vitamin D for immune tolerance, multi-strain probiotic for vaginal+uterine microbiome (*Lactobacillus crispatus* dominance is implantation-friendly)**. **Synthesis:** Receptivity is the soil — gametes are the seeds; both must be optimised.

**2.4 Systemic resilience + EDC detox + nutrient repletion.** Endocrine disruptors (BPA, phthalates, parabens, PFAS, organochlorine pesticides, organophosphates, glyphosate, heavy metals — lead, mercury, cadmium) directly impair oocyte mitochondrial function, sperm DNA integrity, thyroid and ovarian function. Sub-clinical micronutrient deficiencies (iodine, selenium, zinc, iron, B12, folate, choline, vitamin D) are widespread in subfertility cohorts. Targeted by **glass storage, organic produce for Dirty Dozen, clean cosmetics, PFAS-free cookware, water filtration, infrared sauna for sweat-driven EDC elimination, multi-strain probiotic + sprouted millets for gut clearance, full cofactor floor (§6.6)**. **Synthesis:** Without burden reduction + nutrient floor, the other three layers regress.
