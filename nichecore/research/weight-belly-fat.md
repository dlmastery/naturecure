## 1. Executive Summary

Weight gain — especially abdominal / visceral belly fat — is conventionally siloed as "eat less, move more" or, more recently, as a problem solved by GLP-1 agonist injections. The integrative reframing is different: visceral adiposity is the **metabolic-immune-circadian network failure with a dysbiotic gut**, driven by four convergent root mechanisms — (i) insulin resistance + hepatic de-novo lipogenesis + NAFLD/MASLD reinforcing visceral fat storage, (ii) chronic low-grade inflammation from visceral adipose tissue (VAT) secreting IL-6, TNF-α, resistin, leptin and lowering adiponectin, (iii) gut dysbiosis with depleted *Akkermansia*, *Faecalibacterium*, *Bacteroidetes* and elevated *Firmicutes*/Proteobacteria + bile-acid dysregulation + LPS endotoxemia, and (iv) circadian disruption + HPA-axis hyperactivity + leptin/ghrelin dysregulation amplified by ultra-processed food and screen-mediated sleep loss. **Synthesis:** A natural, OTC, home-based protocol that simultaneously restores insulin sensitivity, dampens VAT inflammation, rebuilds the SCFA-producing microbiome and re-anchors the circadian + cortisol axis addresses upstream drivers — not the scale alone — and yields durable belly-fat loss without GLP-1 dependence.

## 2. Mechanistic Network (4-node)

**2.1 Insulin resistance + hepatic de-novo lipogenesis + NAFLD.** Chronic carbohydrate and fructose excess + sedentary behaviour drive hepatic insulin resistance: insulin signalling decouples from glucose disposal while lipogenic ChREBP/SREBP-1c stays on, fuelling VLDL and hepatic triglyceride deposition. Visceral adipocytes hypertrophy, become insulin-resistant themselves, and release FFA + glycerol into portal circulation, worsening hepatic steatosis. Targeted by Berberine HCl / dihydroberberine (AMPK ↑), Cinnamon (insulin sensitiser), *Gymnema sylvestre*, *Momordica charantia* (bitter melon), *Trigonella foenum-graecum* (fenugreek), Inositol, Chromium picolinate, ALA, Milk thistle (silymarin), *Picrorhiza kurroa* (Kutki). **Synthesis:** Reversing hepatic-adipocyte insulin resistance is the upstream brake on belly-fat storage.

**2.2 VAT inflammatory adipokines (leptin / resistin / IL-6 / TNF-α ↑; adiponectin ↓).** Hypertrophic visceral adipocytes recruit M1 macrophages (crown-like structures), secrete pro-inflammatory cytokines that propagate systemic inflammation, impair muscle insulin signalling and depress adiponectin. Targeted by curcumin, Boswellia, omega-3 EPA+DHA, polyphenols (resveratrol, grape seed, pomegranate), green tea EGCG, *Nigella sativa*. **Synthesis:** Reducing VAT inflammation breaks the loop that prevents fat mobilisation.

**2.3 Gut dysbiosis + bile-acid dysregulation + LPS endotoxemia.** Obese metagenomes show low *Akkermansia muciniphila*, *Faecalibacterium prausnitzii*, *Bacteroidetes*; high *Firmicutes*, *Proteobacteria*, methanogenic archaea. SCFA (butyrate / propionate / acetate) drop. LPS translocation via leaky gut chronically activates TLR4 on hepatocytes and adipocytes. Targeted by sprouted Siri Dhanyalu millets, fermented foods, multi-strain probiotic, prebiotic fibres (acacia, GOS, PHGG), pomegranate / cranberry polyphenols (*Akkermansia*-supportive), gluten elimination. **Synthesis:** SCFA recovery is the underlying immune-metabolic lever.

**2.4 Circadian disruption + HPA hyperactivity + leptin/ghrelin dysregulation.** Late-night eating, blue-light exposure, shift work and sleep deprivation flatten cortisol, depress leptin sensitivity, elevate evening ghrelin and amplify visceral storage via SGK1 / 11β-HSD1. Targeted by Ashwagandha KSM-66, *Bacopa*, pranayama, Yoga Nidra, time-restricted eating (10–12 h window), AM sunlight, blue-light cut-off, 18 °C bedroom, cold immersion. **Synthesis:** Without circadian + cortisol normalisation, the other three nodes regress under modern life stress.

### 6.3 Preparation methodology

**Kanji** (probiotic ferment): beetroot 200 g + carrot 100 g + mustard seed 5 g + black salt + 1 L filtered water → ferment 3–5 d at 20–25 °C.

**Dosa batter (gluten-free):** soaked Siri Dhanyalu + urad dal → wet-grind → ferment 12 h.

**Bone broth** (12-h slow simmer) — glycine, collagen, gut-lining support; 200 ml in lieu of mid-afternoon snack.

**Ghee tempering** of bitter greens with cumin and turmeric.

**Vinegar / lemon pre-meal** (1 tbsp) — post-prandial glucose blunting (15–25% AUC reduction in cohort studies).
